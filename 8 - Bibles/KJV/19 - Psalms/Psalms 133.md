###### 1
A Song[^h7892] of degrees[^h4609] of David[^h1732]. Behold, how good[^h2896] and how pleasant[^h5273] it is for brethren[^h251] to dwell[^h3427] together[^h3162] in unity[^h3162]! 
###### 2
It is like the precious[^h2896] ointment[^h8081] upon the head[^h7218], that ran down[^h3381] upon the beard[^h2206], even Aaron's[^h175] beard[^h2206]: that went down[^h3381] to the skirts[^h6310] of his garments[^h4060];
###### 3
As the dew[^h2919] of Hermon[^h2768], and as the dew that descended[^h3381] upon the mountains[^h2042] of Zion[^h6726]: for there the LORD[^h3068] commanded[^h6680] the blessing[^h1293], even life[^h2416] for evermore[^h5769].
