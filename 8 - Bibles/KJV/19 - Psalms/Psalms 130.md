###### 1
A Song[^h7892] of degrees[^h4609]. Out of the depths[^h4615] have I cried[^h7121] unto thee, O LORD[^h3068].
###### 2
Lord[^h136], hear[^h8085] my voice[^h6963]: let thine ears[^h241] be attentive[^h7183] to the voice[^h6963] of my supplications[^h8469].
###### 3
If thou, LORD[^h3050], shouldest mark[^h8104] iniquities[^h5771], O Lord[^h136], who shall stand[^h5975]?
###### 4
But there is forgiveness[^h5547] with thee, that thou mayest be feared[^h3372].
###### 5
I wait[^h6960] for the LORD[^h3068], my soul[^h5315] doth wait[^h6960], and in his word[^h1697] do I hope[^h3176].
###### 6
My soul[^h5315] waiteth for the Lord[^h136] more than they that watch[^h8104] for the morning[^h1242]: I say, more than they that watch[^h8104] for the morning[^h1242]. 
###### 7
Let Israel[^h3478] hope[^h3176] in the LORD[^h3068]: for with the LORD[^h3068] there is mercy[^h2617], and with him is plenteous[^h7235] redemption[^h6304].
###### 8
And he shall redeem[^h6299] Israel[^h3478] from all his iniquities[^h5771].
