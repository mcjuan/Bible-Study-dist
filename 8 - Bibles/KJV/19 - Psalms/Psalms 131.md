###### 1
A Song[^h7892] of degrees[^h4609] of David[^h1732]. LORD[^h3068], my heart[^h3820] is not haughty[^h1361], nor mine eyes[^h5869] lofty[^h7311]: neither do I exercise[^h1980] myself in great matters[^h1419], or in things too high[^h6381] for me.  
###### 2
Surely I have behaved[^h7737] and quieted[^h1826] myself[^h5315], as a child that is weaned[^h1580] of his mother[^h517]: my soul[^h5315] is even as a weaned child[^h1580]. 
###### 3
Let Israel[^h3478] hope[^h3176] in the LORD[^h3068] from henceforth and for[^h5704] ever[^h5769].
