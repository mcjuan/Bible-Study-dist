*A Song of Ascents.*  </i> Behold, bless the LORD, All *you* servants of the LORD, Who by night stand in the house of the LORD!
###### 2
Lift up your hands *in* the sanctuary, And bless the LORD.
###### 3
The LORD who made heaven and earth Bless you from Zion!
