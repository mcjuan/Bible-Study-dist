# G1565 ἐκεῖνος

## ekeînos

_(ek-i'-nos | ek-EE-nose | ake-EE-nose)_

### Definition

from G1563; that one (or (neuter) thing); often intensified by the article prefixed; he, it, the other (same), selfsame, that (same, very), X their, X them, they, this, those; 

- he, she it, etc.
