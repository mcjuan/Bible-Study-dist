# G67 Ἀγρίππας

## Agríppas

_(ag-rip'-pas | ah-GREEP-pahs | ah-GREEP-pahs)_

### Definition

hero-like; apparently from G0066 and G2462; wild-horse tamer; Agrippas, one of the Herods; Agrippa; 

- Name of a ruling family in Israel at the time of Christ
