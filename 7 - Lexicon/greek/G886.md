# G886 ἀχειροποίητος

## acheiropoíētos

_(akh-i-rop-oy'-ay-tos | ah-hee-roh-POO-ay-tose | ah-hee-roh-POO-ay-tose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G5499; unmanufactured, i.e., inartificial; made without (not made with) hands; 

- not made with hands
