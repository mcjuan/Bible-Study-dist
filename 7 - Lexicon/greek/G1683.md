# G1683 ἐμαυτοῦ

## emautoû

_(em-ow-too' | eh-mav-TOO | ay-maf-TOO)_

### Definition

genitive case compound of G1700 and G0846; of myself so likewise the dative case ἐμαυτῷ, and accusative case ἐμαυτόν; me, mine own (self), myself; 

- I, me, myself etc.
