# G627 ἀπολογία

## apología

_(ap-ol-og-ee'-ah | ah-poh-loh-GEE-ah | ah-poh-loh-GEE-ah)_

### Definition

from the same as G0626; a plea ("apology"); answer (for self), clearing of self, defence; 

- verbal defense, speech in defense
- a reasoned statement or argument
