# G1521 εἰσάγω

## eiságō

_(ice-ag'-o | ees-AH-goh | ees-AH-goh)_

### Definition

from G1519 and G0071; to introduce (literally or figuratively); bring in(-to), (+ was to) lead into; 

- to lead in
- to bring in, the place into which not being expressly stated
