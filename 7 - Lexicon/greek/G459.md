# G459 ἄνομος

## ánomos

_(an'-om-os | AH-noh-mose | AH-noh-mose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G3551; lawless, i.e., (negatively) not subject to (the Jewish) law; (by implication, a Gentile), or (positively) wicked; without law, lawless, transgressor, unlawful, wicked; 

- destitute of (the Mosaic) law
  - of the Gentiles
- departing from the law, a violator of the law, lawless, wicked
