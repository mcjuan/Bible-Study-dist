# G926 βαρύς

## barýs

_(bar-ooce' | va-ROOS | va-RYOOS)_

### Definition

from the same as G0922; weighty, i.e., (fig) burdensome, grave; grievous, heavy, weightier; 

- heavy in weight
- metaphorically
  - burdensome
  - severe, stern
  - weighty
    - of great moment
  - violent, cruel, unsparing
