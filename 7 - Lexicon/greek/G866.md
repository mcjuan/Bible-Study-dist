# G866 ἀφιλάργυρος

## aphilárgyros

_(af-il-ar'-goo-ros | ah-fee-LAHR-goo-rose | ah-fee-LAHR-gyoo-rose)_

### Definition

from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G5366; unavaricious; without covetousness, not greedy of filthy lucre; 

- not loving money, not avaricious
