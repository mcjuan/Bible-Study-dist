# G4074 Πέτρος

## Pétros

_(pet'-ros | PEH-trose | PAY-trose)_

### Definition

a rock, a stone; apparently a primary word; a (piece of) rock (larger than G3037); as a name, Petrus, an apostle; Peter, rock; 

- one of the twelve disciples of Jesus

### Note

Compare G2786.
