# G593 ἀποδοκιμάζω

## apodokimázō

_(ap-od-ok-ee-mad'-zo | ah-poh-thoh-kee-MA-zoh | ah-poh-thoh-kee-MA-zoh)_

### Definition

from G0575 and G1381; to disapprove, i.e., (by implication) to repudiate; disallow, reject; 

- to disapprove, reject, repudiate
