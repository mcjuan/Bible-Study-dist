# H6918 קָדוֹשׁ / קדוש

## qâdôwsh

_(kaw-doshe' | kaw-DOHSH | ka-DOHSH)_

### Definition

or קָדֹשׁ; from H6942; sacred (ceremonially or morally); (as noun) God (by eminence), an angel, a saint, a sanctuary; holy (One), saint; a

- sacred, holy, Holy One, saint, set apart
