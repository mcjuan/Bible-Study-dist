# H2658 חָפַר / חפר

## châphar

_(khaw-far' | haw-PAHR | ha-FAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to pry into; by implication, to delve, to explore; dig, paw, search out, seek; v

- to dig, search for
  - (Qal)
    - to dig
    - to search, search out, explore
