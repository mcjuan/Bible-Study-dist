# H4846 מְרֹרָה / מררה

## mᵉrôrâh

_(mer-o-raw' | meh-roh-RAW | meh-roh-RA)_

### Definition

or מְרוֹרָה; from H4843; properly, bitterness; concretely, a bitter thing; specifically bile; also venom (of a serpent); bitter (thing), gall; n-f

- bitter thing, gall, poison
  - gall, gall-bladder (seat of gall)
  - poison, venom
  - bitter thing
  - bitterness
