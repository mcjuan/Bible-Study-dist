# H4341 מַכְאֹב / מכאב

## makʼôb

_(mak-obe' | mahk-OBE | mahk-OVE)_

### Definition

sometimes מַכְאוֹב; also (feminine Isaiah 53:3) מַכְאֹבָה; from H3510; anguish or (figuratively) affliction; grief, pain, sorrow; n-m

- pain, sorrow
  - pain (physical)
  - pain (mental)
