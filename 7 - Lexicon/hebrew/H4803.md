# H4803 מָרַט / מרט

## mâraṭ

_(maw-rat' | maw-RAHT | ma-RAHT)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to polish; by implication, to make bald (the head), to gall (the shoulder); also, to sharpen; bright, furbish, (have his) hair (be) fallen off, peeled, pluck off (hair); v

- to bare, polish, make smooth or bald or bare
  - (Qal)
    - to make bare
    - to scour, polish
  - (Niphal) to be made bald
