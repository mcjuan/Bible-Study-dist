# H367 אֵימָה / אימה

## ʼêymâh

_(ay-maw' | ay-MAW | ay-MA)_

### Definition

or (shortened) אֵמָה; from the same as H0366; fright; concrete, an idol (as a bugbear); dread, fear, horror, idol, terrible, terror; n-f

- terror, dread
