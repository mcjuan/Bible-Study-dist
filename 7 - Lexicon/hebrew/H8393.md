# H8393 תְּבוּאָה / תבואה

## tᵉbûwʼâh

_(teb-oo-aw' | teh-boo-AW | teh-voo-AH)_

### Definition

from H0935; income, i.e., produce (literally or figuratively); fruit, gain, increase, revenue; n-f

- produce, product, revenue
  - product, yield, crops (of the earth usually)
  - income, revenue
  - gain (of wisdom) (figuratively)
  - product of lips (figuratively)
