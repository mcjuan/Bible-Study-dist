# H7892 שִׁיר / שיר

## shîyr

_(sheer | sheer | sheer)_

### Definition

or feminine שִׁירָה; from H7891; a song; abstractly, singing; musical(-ick), × sing(-er, -ing), song; n-m

- (n m) song
  - lyric song
  - religious song
  - song of Levitical choirs
- (n f) song
  - song, ode
