# H5333 נְצִיב / נציב

## nᵉtsîyb

_(nets-eeb' | neh-TSEEB | neh-TSEEV)_

### Definition

or נְצִב; from H5324; something stationary, i.e., a prefect, a military post, a statue; garrison, officer, pillar; n-m

- set over, something placed, pillar, prefect, garrison, post
  - pillar
  - prefect, deputy
