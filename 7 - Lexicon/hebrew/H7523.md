# H7523 רָצַח / רצח

## râtsach

_(raw-tsakh' | raw-TSA | ra-TSAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to dash in pieces, i.e., kill (a human being), especially to murder; put to death, kill, (man-)slay(-er), murder(-er); v

- to murder, slay, kill
  - (Qal) to murder, slay
    - premeditated
    - accidental
    - as avenger
    - slayer (intentional) (participle)
  - (Niphal) to be slain
  - (Piel)
    - to murder, assassinate
    - murderer, assassin (participle)(subst)
  - (Pual) to be killed
