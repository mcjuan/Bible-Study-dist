# H70 אֹבֶן / אבן

## ʼôben

_(o' ben | oh-BEN | oh-VEN)_

### Definition

from the same as H0068; a pair of stones (only dual); a potter's wheel or a midwife's stool (consisting alike of two horizontal disks with a support between); wheel, stool; n-m

- wheel, disc
  - potter's wheel
  - bearing-stool, midwife's stool
