# H2519 חֲלַקְלַקָּה / חלקלקה

## chălaqlaqqâh

_(khal-ak-lak-kaw' | huh-lahk-lahk-KAW | huh-lahk-la-KA)_

### Definition

by reduplication from H2505; properly, something very smooth; i.e., a treacherous spot; figuratively, blandishment; flattery, slippery; n-f

- flattery, slipperiness, fine promises, smoothness
  - slipperiness
  - fine promises
