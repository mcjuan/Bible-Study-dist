# H2889 טָהוֹר / טהור

## ṭâhôwr

_(taw-hore' | taw-HORE | ta-HORE)_

### Definition

or טָהֹר; from H2891; pure (in a physical, chemical, ceremonial or moral sense); clean, fair, pure(-ness); a

- pure, clean
  - clean (ceremonially—of animals)
  - pure (physically)
  - pure, clean (morally, ethically)
