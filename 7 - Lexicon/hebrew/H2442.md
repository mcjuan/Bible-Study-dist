# H2442 חָכָה / חכה

## châkâh

_(khaw-kaw' | haw-KAW | ha-HA)_

### Definition

a primitive root (apparently akin to H2707 through the idea of piercing); properly, to adhere to; hence, to await; long, tarry, wait; v

- to wait, wait for, await
  - (Qal) to wait for
  - (Piel)
    - to wait, tarry
    - to wait (in ambush)
    - to wait for, long for
