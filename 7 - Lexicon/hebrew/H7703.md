# H7703 שָׁדַד / שדד

## shâdad

_(shaw-dad' | shaw-DAHD | sha-DAHD)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to be burly, i.e., (figuratively) powerful (passively, impregnable); by implication, to ravage; dead, destroy(-er), oppress, robber, spoil(-er), × utterly, (lay) waste; v

- to deal violently with, despoil, devastate, ruin, destroy, spoil
  - (Qal)
    - to violently destroy, devastate, despoil, assail
    - devastator, despoiler (participle) (subst)
  - (Niphal) to be utterly ruined
  - (Piel)
    - to assault
    - to devastate
  - (Pual) to be devastated
  - (Poel) to violently destroy
  - (Hophal) to be devastated
