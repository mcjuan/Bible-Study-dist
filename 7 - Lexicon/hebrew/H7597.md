# H7597 שְׁאַלְתִּיאֵל / שאלתיאל

## Shᵉʼaltîyʼêl

_(sheh-al-tee-ale' | sheh-al-tee-ALE | sheh-al-tee-ALE)_

### Definition

I have asked of God; or שַׁלְתִּיאֵל; from H7592 and H0410; I have asked God; Shealtiel, an Israelite; Shalthiel, Shealtiel; n-pr-m

- father of Zerubbabel
- son of king Jehoiachin or Jeconiah or Coniah of Judah and uncle of Zerubbabel
