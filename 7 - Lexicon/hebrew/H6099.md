_(aw-tsoom' | aw-TSOOM | ah-TSOOM)_

### Definition

or עָצֻם; passive participle of H6105; powerful (specifically, a paw); by implication, numerous; + feeble, great, mighty, must, strong; a

- mighty, vast, numerous
  - mighty, strong (in number)
  - numerous, countless
