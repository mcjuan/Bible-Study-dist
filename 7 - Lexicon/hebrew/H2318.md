# H2318 חָדַשׁ / חדש

## châdash

_(khaw-dash' | haw-DAHSH | ha-DAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to be new; causatively, to rebuild; renew, repair; v

- to be new, renew, repair
  - (Piel)
    - to renew, make anew
    - to repair
  - (Hithpael) to renew oneself
