# H8138 שָׁנָה / שנה

## shânâh

_(shaw-naw' | shaw-NAW | sha-NA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to fold, i.e., duplicate (literally or figuratively); by implication, to transmute (transitive or intransitive); do (speak, strike) again, alter, double, (be given to) change, disguise, (be) diverse, pervert, prefer, repeat, return, do the second time; v

- to repeat, do again, change, alter
  - (Qal) to change
  - (Niphal) to be repeated
  - (Piel) to change, alter
  - (Hithpael) to disguise oneself
