# H2522 חָלַשׁ / חלש

## châlash

_(khaw-lash' | haw-LAHSH | ha-LAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to prostrate; by implication, to overthrow, decay; discomfit, waste away, weaken; v

- to be weak, be prostrate
  - (Qal) to be prostrate
- to weaken, disable, prostrate
  - (Qal) to disable, prostrate
