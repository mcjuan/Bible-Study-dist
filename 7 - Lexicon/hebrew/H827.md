# H827 אַשְׁפָּה / אשפה

## ʼashpâh

_(ash-paw' | ash-PAW | ash-PA)_

### Definition

perhaps (feminine) from the same as H0825 (in the sense of covering); a quiver or arrow-case; quiver; n-f

- quiver (for arrows)
  - of home, of God's instruments (figuratively)
