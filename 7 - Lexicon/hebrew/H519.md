# H519 אָמָה / אמה

## ʼâmâh

_(aw-maw' | aw-MAW | ah-MA)_

### Definition

apparently a primitive word; a maid-servant or female slave; (hand-)bondmaid(-woman), maid(-servant); n-f

- maid-servant, female slave, maid, handmaid, concubine
  - of humility (figuratively)
