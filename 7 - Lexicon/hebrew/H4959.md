# H4959 מָשַׁשׁ / משש

## mâshash

_(maw-shash' | maw-SHAHSH | ma-SHAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to feel of; by implication, to grope; feel, grope, search; v

- to feel, grope
  - (Qal) to feel, grope
  - (Piel) to feel over or through, grope
    - to feel through
    - to grope
  - (Hiphil) to feel
