# H784 אֵשׁ / אש

## ʼêsh

_(aysh | aysh | aysh)_

### Definition

a primitive word; fire (literally or figuratively); burning, fiery, fire, flaming, hot; n-f

- fire
  - fire, flames
  - supernatural fire (accompanying theophany)
  - fire (for cooking, roasting, parching)
  - altar-fire
  - God's anger (figuratively)
