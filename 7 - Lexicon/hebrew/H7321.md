# H7321 רוּעַ / רוע

## rûwaʻ

_(roo-ah' | ROO-ah | ROO-ah)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to mar (especially by breaking); figuratively, to split the ears (with sound), i.e., shout (for alarm or joy); blow an alarm, cry (alarm, aloud, out), destroy, make a joyful noise, smart, shout (for joy), sound an alarm, triumph; v

- to shout, raise a sound, cry out, give a blast
  - (Hiphil)
    - to shout a war-cry or alarm of battle
    - to sound a signal for war or march
    - to shout in triumph (over enemies)
    - to shout in applause
    - to shout (with religious impulse)
    - to cry out in distress
  - (Polal) to utter a shout
  - (Hithpolel)
    - to shout in triumph
    - to shout for joy
- (Niphal) destroyed
