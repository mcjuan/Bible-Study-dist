# H7257 רָבַץ / רבץ

## râbats

_(raw-bats' | raw-BAHTS | ra-VAHTS)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to crouch (on all four legs folded, like a recumbent animal); by implication, to recline, repose, brood, lurk, imbed; crouch (down), fall down, make a fold, lay, (cause to, make to) lie (down), make to rest, sit; v

- to stretch oneself out, lie down, lie stretched out
  - (Qal) to lie down, lie
  - (Hiphil) to cause to lie down
    - laying (stones)
