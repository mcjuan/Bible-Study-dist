# H6884 צָרַף / צרף

## tsâraph

_(tsaw-raf' | tsaw-RAHP | tsa-RAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to fuse (metal), i.e., refine (literally or figuratively); cast, (re-)fine(-er), founder, goldsmith, melt, pure, purge away, try; v

- to smelt, refine, test
  - (Qal)
    - to smelt, refine
    - to test
    - to test (and prove true)
    - smelter, refiner, goldsmith (participle)
  - (Niphal) to be refined
  - (Piel) to be a refiner
    - refiner (participle)
