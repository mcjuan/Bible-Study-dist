# H4794 מְרוּצָה / מרוצה

## mᵉrûwtsâh

_(mer-oo-tsaw' | meh-roo-TSAW | meh-roo-TSA)_

### Definition

or מְרֻצָה; feminine of H4793; a race (the act), whether the manner or the progress; course, running; n-f

- running, course (of life)
  - running, mode or style of running
  - course (of life)
