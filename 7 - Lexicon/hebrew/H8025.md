# H8025 שָׁלַף / שלף

## shâlaph

_(saw-laf' | shaw-LAHP | sha-LAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to pull out, up or off; draw (off), grow up, pluck off; v

- to draw out or off, take off
  - (Qal)
    - to draw out
    - to draw off
    - to shoot up, draw out (of blade of grass on roof)
