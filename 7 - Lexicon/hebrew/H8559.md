# H8559 תָּמָר / תמר

## Tâmâr

_(taw-mawr' | taw-MAWR | ta-MAHR)_

### Definition

palm-tree; the same as H8558; Tamar, the name of three women and a place; Tamar; n-pr

- (n pr f) widow of Er, the son of Judah; fiancee of Shelah, another son of Judah; wife of Judah and mother of Pharez and Zerah
- daughter of David by Maacah, sister of Absalom, and rape victim of Amnon her half brother, another son of David
- daughter of Absalom
- (n pr loc) a place on the southern border of Gad
