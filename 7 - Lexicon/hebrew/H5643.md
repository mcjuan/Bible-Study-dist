# H5643 סֵתֶר / סתר

## çêther

_(say'-ther | say-TER | say-TER)_

### Definition

or (feminine) סִתְרָה; (Deuteronomy 32:38), from H5641; a cover (in a good or a bad, a literal or a figurative sense); backbiting, covering, covert, × disguise(-th), hiding place, privily, protection, secret(-ly) (place); n-m

- (n m) covering, shelter, hiding place, secrecy
  - covering, cover
  - hiding place, shelter, secret place
  - secrecy
    - secrecy (of tongue being slanderous)
- (n f) shelter, protection
