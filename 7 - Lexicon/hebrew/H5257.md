# H5257 נְסִיךְ / נסיך

## nᵉçîyk

_(nes-eek' | neh-SEEK | neh-SEEK)_

### Definition

from H5258; properly, something poured out, i.e., a libation; by implication, a prince (as anointed); drink offering, duke, prince(-ipal); n-m

- poured out, libation, molten image, one anointed
  - libation, drink-offering
  - molten image
- prince, anointed one
