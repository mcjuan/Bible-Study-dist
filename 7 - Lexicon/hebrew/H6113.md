# H6113 עָצָר / עצר

## ʻâtsâr

_(aw-tsar' | aw-TSAWR | ah-TSAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to inclose; by analogy, to hold back; also to maintain, rule, assemble; × be able, close up, detain, fast, keep (self close, still), prevail, recover, refrain, × reign, restrain, retain, shut (up), slack, stay, stop, withhold (self); v

- to restrain, retain, close up, shut, withhold, refrain, stay, detain
  - (Qal)
    - to restrain, halt, stop
    - to retain
  - (Niphal) to be restrained, be stayed, be under restraint
