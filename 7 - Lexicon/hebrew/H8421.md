# H8421 תּוּב / תוב

## tûwb

_(toob | toob | toov)_

### Definition

corresponding to H7725, to come back; specifically (transitive and ellip.) to reply; answer, restore, return (an answer); v

- to return, come back
  - (P'al) to return, come back
  - (Aphel)
    - to restore, give back, answer
    - to return
