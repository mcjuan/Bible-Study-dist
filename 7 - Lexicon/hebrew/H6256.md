# H6256 עֵת / עת

## ʻêth

_(ayth | ate | ate)_

### Definition

from H5703; time, especially (adverb with preposition) now, when, etc; + after, (al-)ways, × certain, + continually, + evening, long, (due) season, so (long) as, (even-, evening-, noon-)tide, (what) (meal-)time, when; n-f

- time
  - time (of an event)
  - time (usual)
  - experiences, fortunes
  - occurrence, occasion
