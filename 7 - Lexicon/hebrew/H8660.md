# H8660 תִּרְשָׁתָא / תרשתא

## Tirshâthâʼ

_(teer-shaw-thaw' | tir-shaw-TAW | teer-sha-TA)_

### Definition

of foreign derivation; Tirshatha, the title of a Persian deputy or governor; Tirshatha; n-m

- Tirshatha—governor
  - a title used by the Persian governor in Judea
    - Nehemiah used this title
