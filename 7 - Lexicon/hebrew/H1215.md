## betsaʻ

_(beh'-tsah | beh-TSA | beh-TSA)_

### Definition

from H1214; plunder; by extension, gain (usually unjust); covetousness, (dishonest) gain, lucre, profit; n-m

- profit, unjust gain, gain (profit) acquired by violence
