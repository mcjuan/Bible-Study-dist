# H7123 קְרָא / קרא

## qᵉrâʼ

_(ker-aw' | keh-RAW | keh-RA)_

### Definition

corresponding to H7121; to call out to (i.e., properly, address by name, but used in a wide variety of applications); call, cry, read; v

- to call, read aloud, read out, shout
  - (P'al)
    - to call, proclaim
    - to read aloud, read out
  - (Ithp'el) to be summoned, be called
