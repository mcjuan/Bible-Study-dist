# H2263 חָבַק / חבק

## châbaq

_(khaw-bak' | haw-BAHK | ha-VAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to clasp (the hands or in embrace); embrace, fold; v

- to embrace, clasp
  - (Qal)
    - to embrace
    - to fold one's hands in idleness (figuratively)
  - (Piel) to embrace
