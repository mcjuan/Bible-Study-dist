# H5344 נָקַב / נקב

## nâqab

_(naw-kab' | naw-KAHB | na-KAHV)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to puncture, literally (to perforate, with more or less violence) or figuratively (to specify, designate, libel); appoint, blaspheme, bore, curse, express, with holes, name, pierce, strike through; v

- to pierce, perforate, bore, appoint
  - (Qal)
    - to pierce, bore
    - to prick off, designate
  - (Niphal) to be pricked off, be designated, be specified
- (Qal) to curse, blaspheme
