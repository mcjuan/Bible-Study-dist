# H2331 חָוָה / חוה

## châvâh

_(khaw-vah' | haw-WAW | ha-VA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; (compare H2324, H2421); properly, to live; by implication (intensively) to declare or show; show; v

- (Piel) to tell, declare, show, make known
- to breath
