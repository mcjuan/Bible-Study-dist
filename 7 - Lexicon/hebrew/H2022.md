# H2022 הַר / הר

## har

_(har | hahr | hahr)_

### Definition

a shortened form of H2042; a mountain or range of hills (sometimes used figuratively); hill (country), mount(-ain), × promotion; n-m

- hill, mountain, hill country, mount
