# H8274 שֵׁרֵבְיָה / שרביה

## Shêrêbᵉyâh

_(shay-rayb-yaw' | shay-ray-beh-YAW | shay-ray-veh-YA)_

### Definition

Jehovah has scorched; from H8273 and H3050; Jah has brought heat; Sherebjah, the name of two Israelites; Sherebiah; n-pr-m

- a Levite who assisted Ezra at the reading of the Law and who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
