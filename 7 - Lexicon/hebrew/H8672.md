# H8672 תֵּשַׁע / תשע

## têshaʻ

_(tay'-shah | tay-SHA | tay-SHA)_

### Definition

or (masculine) תִּשְׁעָה; perhaps from H8159 through the idea of a turn to the next or full number ten; nine or (ordinal) ninth; nine, ninth, + nine(-teen(-th)); n

- nine, nonad
  - nine (as cardinal number)
  - ninth (as ordinal number)
  - in combination with other numbers
