# H8602 תָּפֵל / תפל

## tâphêl

_(taw-fale' | taw-PALE | ta-FALE)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to smear; plaster (as gummy) or slime; (figuratively) frivolity; foolish things, unsavoury, untempered; a

- (adj) foolish, insipid
- tasteless, unseasoned
  - meaning uncertain
- (n m) whitewash
