# H5959 עַלְמָה / עלמה

## ʻalmâh

_(al-maw' | al-MAW | al-MA)_

### Definition

feminine of H5958; a lass (as veiled or private); damsel, maid, virgin; n-f

- virgin

## Note

There is no instance where it can be proved that this word describes a young woman who is not a virgin.
