# H6418 פֶּלֶךְ / פלך

## pelek

_(peh'-lek | peh-LEK | peh-LEK)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to be round; a circuit (i.e., district); also a spindle (as whirled); hence, a crutch; (di-)staff, participle; n-m

- whirl of spindle, stick, district
  - whirl of spindle, stick
  - district, circuit
