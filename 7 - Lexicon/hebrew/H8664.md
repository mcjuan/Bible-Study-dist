# H8664 תִּשְׁבִּי / תשבי

## Tishbîy

_(tish-bee' | tish-BEE | teesh-BEE)_

### Definition

captivity; patrial from an unused name meaning recourse; a Tishbite or inhabitant of Tishbeh (in Gilead); Tishbite; n-pr

- (n pr loc) the home of Elijah
  - site unknown but maybe the town of Tishbe
- (adj gent) an inhabitant of Tishbe or Tishbi or Tesheb
  - site unknown and name uncertain
