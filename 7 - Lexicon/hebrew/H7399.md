# H7399 רְכוּשׁ / רכוש

## rᵉkûwsh

_(rek-oosh' | reh-KOOSH | reh-HOOSH)_

### Definition

or רְכֻשׁ; from passive participle of H7408; property (as gathered); good, riches, substance; n-m

- property, goods, possessions
  - property, goods
    - general term for all movable goods
    - of livestock
    - of stores, utensils, etc
