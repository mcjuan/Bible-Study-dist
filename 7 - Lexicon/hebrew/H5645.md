# H5645 עָב / עב

## ʻâb

_(awb | awb | av)_

### Definition

(masculine and feminine); from H5743; properly, an envelope, i.e., darkness (or density, 2 Chronicles 4:17); specifically, a (scud) cloud; also a copse; clay, (thick) cloud, × thick, thicket; n-m

- darkness, cloud, thicket
  - dark cloud
  - cloud mass
  - thicket (as refuge)

## Note

Compare H5672.
