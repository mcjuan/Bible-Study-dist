# H6499 פַּר / פר

## par

_(par | pahr | pahr)_

### Definition

or פָּר; from H6565; a bullock (apparently as breaking forth in wild strength, or perhaps as dividing the hoof); (+ young) bull(-ock), calf, ox; n-m

- young bull, steer, bullock
