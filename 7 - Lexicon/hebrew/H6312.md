# H6312 פּוּאָה / פואה

## Pûwʼâh

_(poo-aw' | poo-AW | poo-AH)_

### Definition

splendid; or פֻּוָּה; from H6284; a blast; Puah or Puvvah, the name of two Israelites; Phuvah, Pua, Puah; n-pr-m

- son of Issachar
  - also 'Pua' and 'Phuvah'
- man of Issachar, father of Tola, the judge of Israel after Abimelech
