# H6084 עׇפְרָה / עפרה

## ʻOphrâh

_(of-raw' | ope-RAW | ofe-RA)_

### Definition

fawn; feminine of H6082; female fawn; Ophrah, the name of an Israelite and of two places in Palestine; Ophrah; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a Judaite, son of Meonothai
- (n pr loc) a town of Benjamin approximately 5 miles (8 km) east of Bethel
- a place in Manasseh, native place of Gideon; probably located close to Shechem
