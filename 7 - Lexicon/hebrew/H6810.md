# H6810 צָעִיר / צעיר

## tsâʻîyr

_(tsaw-eer' | tsaw-EER | tsa-EER)_

### Definition

or צָעוֹר; from H6819; little; (in number) few; (in age) young, (in value) ignoble; least, little (one), small (one), + young(-er, -est); a

- little, insignificant, young
  - little, insignificant
  - insignificant, mean
  - young, younger, youngest
