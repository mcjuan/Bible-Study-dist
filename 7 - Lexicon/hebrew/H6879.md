# H6879 צָרַע / צרע

## tsâraʻ

_(tsaw-rah' | tsaw-RA | tsa-RA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to scourge, i.e., (intransitive and figurative) to be stricken with leprosy; leper, leprous; v

- to be diseased of skin, be leprous
  - (Qal) to be a leper
  - (Pual) to have leprosy
