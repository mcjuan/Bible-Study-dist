# H7614 שְׁבָא / שבא

## Shᵉbâʼ

_(sheb-aw' | sheh-BAW | sheh-VA)_

### Definition

seven, an oath; of foreign origin; Sheba, the name of three early progenitors of tribes and of an Ethiopian district; Sheba, Sabeans; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) son of Joktan and a descendant of Seth
- son of Raamah, grandson of Cush, and a descendant of Ham
- son of Jokshan, the son of Abraham by Keturah
- (n pr loc) a nation in southern Arabia
