## tachtîy

_(takh-tee' | ta-TEE | tahk-TEE)_

### Definition

from H8478; lowermost; as noun (feminine plural) the depths (figuratively, a pit, the womb); low (parts, -er, -er parts, -est), nether (part); a

- low, lower, lowest
  - lowest (as adj)
  - the lower parts (subst)
