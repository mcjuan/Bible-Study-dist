# H7448 רֶסֶן / רסן

## reçen

_(reh'-sen | reh-SEN | reh-SEN)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to curb; a halter (as restraining); by implication, the jaw; bridle; n-m

- something that restrains, halter, jaw
  - halter
    - of restraint thrown off (figuratively)
  - jaw (of crocodile)
