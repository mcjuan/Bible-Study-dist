# H1442 גָּדַף / גדף

## gâdaph

_(gaw-daf' | ɡaw-DAHP | ɡa-DAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to hack (with words), i.e., revile; blaspheme, reproach; v

- to revile men, blaspheme God
  - (Piel)
    - to revile (between men)
    - to blaspheme (God)
