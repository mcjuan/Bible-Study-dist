# H5080 נָדַח / נדח

## nâdach

_(naw-dakh' | naw-DA | na-DAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to push off; used in a great variety of applications, literally and figuratively (to expel, mislead, strike, inflict, etc.); banish, bring, cast down (out), chase, compel, draw away, drive (away, out, quite), fetch a stroke, force, go away, outcast, thrust away (out), withdraw; v

- to impel, thrust, drive away, banish
  - (Qal)
    - to impel
    - to thrust away, banish
  - (Niphal)
    - to be impelled
    - to be thrust out, be banished
      - banished, outcast (participle)
    - to be driven away
    - to be thrust away, thrust aside
  - (Pual) to be thrust into
    - thrust (participle)
  - (Hiphil)
    - to thrust, move, impel
    - to thrust out, banish
    - to thrust away, thrust aside
  - (Hophal) to be chased, be hunted
    - chased, hunted (participle)
