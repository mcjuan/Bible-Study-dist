# H6159 עֹרֵב / ערב

## ʻÔrêb

_(o-rabe' | oh-RABE | oh-RAVE)_

### Definition

raven; or עוֹרֵב; the same as H6158; Oreb, the name of a Midianite and of the cliff near the Jordan; Oreb; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) one of the chieftains of the Midianite army defeated by Gideon
- (n pr loc) a rock east of the Jordan where Oreb and part of his army fell to the Ephraimites
