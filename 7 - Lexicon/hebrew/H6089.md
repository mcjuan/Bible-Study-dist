# H6089 עֶצֶב / עצב

## ʻetseb

_(eh'-tseb | eh-TSEB | eh-TSEV)_

### Definition

from H6087; an earthen vessel; usually (painful) toil; also a pang (whether of body or mind); grievous, idol, labor, sorrow; n-m

- pain, hurt, toil, sorrow, labor, hardship
  - pain
  - hurt, offense
  - toil, hardship
- vessel, creation, object
- idol
