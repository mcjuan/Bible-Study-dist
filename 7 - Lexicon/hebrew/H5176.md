# H5176 נָחָשׁ / נחש

## Nâchâsh

_(naw-khawsh' | naw-HOHSH | na-HOHSH)_

### Definition

serpent; the same as H5175; Nachash, the name of two persons apparently non-Israelite; Nahash; n-pr-m

- (n pr m) a king of the Ammonites during the time of king Saul
- the father of Abigail, the mother of Amasa, the commander of Absalom's army
