# H5737 עֲדַר / עדר

## ʻădar

_(aw-dar' | uh-DAHR | uh-DAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to arrange, as a battle, a vineyard (to hoe); hence, to muster and so to miss (or find wanting); dig, fail, keep (rank), lack; v

- to help
  - (Qal) to help
- to hoe
  - (Niphal) to be hoed
- to be lacking, fail
  - (Niphal)
  - (Piel) to leave lacking
    - to leave lacking
