# H5971 עַם / עם

## ʻam

_(am | am | am)_

### Definition

from H6004; a people (as a congregated unit); specifically, a tribe (as those of Israel); hence (collectively) troops or attendants; figuratively, a flock; folk, men, nation, people; n-m

- nation, people
  - people, nation
  - persons, members of one's people, compatriots, country-men
- kinsman, kindred
