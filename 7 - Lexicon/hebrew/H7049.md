# H7049 קָלַע / קלע

## qâlaʻ

_(kaw-lah' | kaw-LA | ka-LA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to sling; also to carve (as if a circular motion, or into light forms); carve, sling (out); v

- to sling, hurl forth
  - (Qal)
    - to sling
    - slinger (participle)
  - (Piel) to sling
- (Qal) to carve
