# H6998 קָטַף / קטף

## qâṭaph

_(kaw-taf' | kaw-TAHP | ka-TAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to strip off; crop off, cut down (up), pluck; v

- to pluck off or out, cut off
  - (Qal) to pluck off
  - (Niphal) to be plucked off
