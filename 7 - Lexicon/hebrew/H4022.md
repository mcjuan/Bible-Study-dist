# H4022 מֶגֶד / מגד

## meged

_(meh'-ghed | meh-ɡED | meh-ɡED)_

### Definition

from an unused root probably meaning to be eminent; properly, a distinguished thing; hence something valuable, as a product or fruit; pleasant, precious fruit (thing); n-m

- excellence
