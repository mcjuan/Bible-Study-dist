# H4889 מַשְׁחִית / משחית

## mashchîyth

_(mash-kheeth' | mahsh-HEET | mahsh-HEET)_

### Definition

from H7843; destructive, i.e., (as noun) destruction, literally (specifically a snare) or figuratively (corruption); corruption, (to) destroy(-ing), destruction, trap, × utterly; n-m

- ruin, destruction
