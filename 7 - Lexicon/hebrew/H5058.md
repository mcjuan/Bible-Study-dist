# H5058 נְגִינָה / נגינה

## nᵉgîynâh

_(neg-ee-naw' | neh-ɡee-NAW | neh-ɡee-NA)_

### Definition

or נְגִינַת; (Psalm 61:title), from H5059; properly, instrumental music; by implication, a stringed instrument; by extension, a poem set to music; specifically, an epigram; stringed instrument, musick, Neginoth (plural), song; n-f

- music, song, taunt song
  - music (of stringed instrument)
  - song
    - taunting or mocking song
