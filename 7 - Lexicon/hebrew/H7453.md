# H7453 רֵעַ / רע

## rêaʻ

_(ray'-ah | RAY-ah | RAY-ah)_

### Definition

or רֵיעַ; from H7462; an associate (more or less close); brother, companion, fellow, friend, husband, lover, neighbour, × (an-)other; n-m

- friend, companion, fellow, another person
  - friend, intimate
  - fellow, fellow-citizen, another person (weaker sense)
  - other, another (reciprocal phrase)
