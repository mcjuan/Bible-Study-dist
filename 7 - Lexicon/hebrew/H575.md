# H575 אָן / אן

## ʼân

_(awn | awn | an)_

### Definition

or אָנָה; contracted from H0370; where?; hence, whither?, when?; also hither and thither; + any (no) whither, now, where, whither(-soever); adv

- where?, whither? (of place)
- when?, until when?, how long? (of time)
