# H4743 מָקַק / מקק

## mâqaq

_(maw-kak' | maw-KAHK | ma-KAHK)_

### Definition

' a primitive root; to melt; figuratively, to flow, dwindle, vanish; consume away, be corrupt, dissolve, pine away; v

- to decay, pine away, rot, fester
  - (Niphal)
    - to fester (of wounds)
    - to rot, rot away
    - to moulder away
    - to pine away
  - (Hiphil) to cause to rot
