# H4744 מִקְרָא / מקרא

## miqrâʼ

_(mik-raw' | mik-RAW | meek-RA)_

### Definition

from H7121; something called out, i.e., a public meeting (the act, the persons, or the place); also a rehearsal; assembly, calling, convocation, reading; n-m

- convocation, convoking, reading, a calling together
  - convocation, sacred assembly
  - convoking
  - reading
