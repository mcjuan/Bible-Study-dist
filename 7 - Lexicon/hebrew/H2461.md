# H2461 חָלָב / חלב

## châlâb

_(khaw-lawb' | haw-LAWB | ha-LAHV)_

### Definition

from the same as H2459; milk (as the richness of kine); + cheese, milk, sucking; n-m

- milk, sour milk, cheese
  - milk
  - abundance of the land (metaphorically)
  - white (as milk)
