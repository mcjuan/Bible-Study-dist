# H1534 גַּלְגַּל / גלגל

## galgal

_(gal-gal' | ɡahl-ɡAHL | ɡahl-ɡAHL)_

### Definition

by reduplication from H1556; a wheel; by analogy, a whirlwind; also dust (as whirled); heaven, rolling thing, wheel; n-m

- wheel, whirl, whirlwind, whirling
  - wheel
  - whirl (of dust, chaff)
