# H5107 נוּב / נוב

## nûwb

_(noob | noob | noov)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to germinate, i.e., (figuratively) to (causatively, make) flourish; also (of words), to utter; bring forth (fruit), make cheerful, increase; v

- to bear fruit
  - (Qal) to bear fruit
  - (Pilel) to make to flourish (figuratively)
