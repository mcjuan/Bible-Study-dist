# H5713 עֵדָה / עדה

## ʻêdâh

_(ay-daw' | ay-DAW | ay-DA)_

### Definition

feminine of H5707 in its technical sense; testimony; testimony, witness; n-f

- testimony, witness
  - always plural and always of laws as divine testimonies
