# H7600 שַׁאֲנָן / שאנן

## shaʼănân

_(shah-an-awn' | sha-uh-NAWN | sha-uh-NAHN)_

### Definition

from H7599; secure; in a bad sense, haughty; that is at ease, quiet, tumult; a

- (adj) at ease, quiet, secure
  - at ease, secure
  - at ease, careless, wanton, arrogant
- (subst) security, pride, arrogance
  - one at ease
  - arrogance
