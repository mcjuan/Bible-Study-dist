# H1160 בְּעוֹר / בעור

## Bᵉʻôwr

_(beh-ore' | beh-ORE | beh-ORE)_

### Definition

burning; from H1197 (in the sense of burning); a lamp; Beor, the name of the father of an Edomitish king; also of that of Balaam; Beor; n-pr-m

- father of Balaam
- father of Bela, king of Edom
