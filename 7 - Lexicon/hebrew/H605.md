# H605 אָנַשׁ / אנש

## ʼânash

_(aw-nash' | aw-NAHSH | ah-NAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to be frail, feeble, or (figuratively) melancholy; desperate(-ly wicked), incurable, sick, woeful; v

- to be weak, sick, frail
  - (Qal)
    - to be incurable
    - to be sick
    - desperate, incurable, desperately wicked, woeful, very sick (pass participle) (metaphorically)
  - (Niphal) to be sick
