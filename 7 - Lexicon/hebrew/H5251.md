_(nace | nase | nase)_

### Definition

from H5264; a flag; also a sail; by implication, a flagstaff; generally a signal; figuratively, a token; banner, pole, sail, (en-)sign, standard; n-m

- something lifted up, standard, signal, signal pole, ensign, banner, sign, sail
  - standard (as rallying point), signal
  - standard (pole)
  - ensign, signal
