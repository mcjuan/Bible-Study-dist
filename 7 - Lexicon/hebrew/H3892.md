# H3892 לַח / לח

## lach

_(lakh | la | lahk)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to be new; fresh, i.e., unused or undried; green, moist; a

- moist, fresh, new
  - moist, fresh (of plants)
  - new (of cords, sinews)
