# H4687 מִצְוָה / מצוה

## mitsvâh

_(mits-vaw' | mits-WAW | meets-VA)_

### Definition

from H6680; a command, whether human or divine (collectively, the Law); (which was) commanded(-ment), law, ordinance, precept; n-f

- commandment
  - commandment (of man)
  - the commandment (of God)
  - commandment (of code of wisdom)
