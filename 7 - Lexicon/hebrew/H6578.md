# H6578 פְּרָת / פרת

## Pᵉrâth

_(per-awth' | peh-RAWT | peh-RAHT)_

### Definition

fruitfulness; from an unused root meaning to break forth; rushing; Perath (i.e., Euphrates), a river of the East; Euphrates; n-pr-m

- the largest and longest river of western Asia; rises from two chief sources in the Armenian mountains and flows into the Persian Gulf
