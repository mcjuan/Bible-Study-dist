# H5066 נָגַשׁ / נגש

## nâgash

_(naw-gash' | naw-ɡAHSH | na-ɡAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to be or come (causatively, bring) near (for any purpose); euphemistically, to lie with a woman; as an enemy, to attack; religious to worship; causatively, to present; figuratively, to adduce an argument; by reversal, to stand back; (make to) approach (nigh), bring (forth, hither, near), (cause to) come (hither, near, nigh), give place, go hard (up), (be, draw, go) near (nigh), offer, overtake, present, put, stand; v

- to draw near, approach
  - (Qal) to draw or come near
    - of humans
      - of sexual intercourse
    - of inanimate subject
      - to approach one another
  - (Niphal) to draw near
  - (Hiphil) to cause to approach, bring near, bring
  - (Hophal) to be brought near
  - (Hithpael) to draw near
