# H4679 מְצַד / מצד

## mᵉtsad

_(mets-ad' | meh-TSAHD | meh-TSAHD)_

### Definition

or מְצָד; or (feminine) מְצָדָה; from H6679; a fastness (as a covert of ambush); castle, fort, (strong) hold, munition; n-f

- fort, stronghold, fastness
  - mountain-fastness
  - stronghold
