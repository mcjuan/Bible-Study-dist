# H8147 שְׁנַיִם / שנים

## shᵉnayim

_(shen-ah'-yim | sheh-na-YIM | sheh-na-YEEM)_

### Definition

dual of H8145; feminine שְׁתַּיִם; two; also (as ordinal) twofold; both, couple, double, second, twain, + twelfth, + twelve, + twenty (sixscore) thousand, twice, two; n

- two
  - two (the cardinal number)
    - two, both, double, twice
  - second (the ordinal number)
  - in combination with other numbers
  - both (a dual number)
