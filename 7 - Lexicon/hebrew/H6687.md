# H6687 צוּף / צוף

## tsûwph

_(tsoof | tsoop | tsoof)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to overflow; (make to over-)flow, swim; v

- to flow, overflow, flood, float
  - (Qal) to flood
  - (Hiphil)
    - to cause to flow over
    - to cause to float
