# H4688 מְצוֹלָה / מצולה

## mᵉtsôwlâh

_(mets-o-law' | meh-tsoh-LAW | meh-tsoh-LA)_

### Definition

or מְצֹלָה; also מְצוּלָה; or מְצֻלָה; from the same as H6683; a deep place (of water or mud); bottom, deep, depth; n-f

- depth, the deep, the deep sea
