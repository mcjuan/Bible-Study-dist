# H6754 צֶלֶם / צלם

## tselem

_(tseh'-lem | tseh-LEM | tseh-LEM)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to shade; a phantom, i.e., (figuratively) illusion, resemblance; hence, a representative figure, especially an idol; image, vain shew; n-m

- image
  - images (of tumours, mice, heathen gods)
  - image, likeness (of resemblance)
  - mere, empty, image, semblance (figuratively)
