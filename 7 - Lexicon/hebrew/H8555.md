# H8555 תִּמְנָע / תמנע

## Timnâʻ

_(tim-naw' | tim-NAW | teem-NA)_

### Definition

restrained; from H4513; restraint; Timna, the name of two Edomites; Timna, Timnah; n-pr-m n-pr-f

- (n pr m) a chief or duke of Esau and of Edom
- (n pr f) the concubine of Eliphaz, the son of Esau
- daughter of Seir the Horite and sister of Lotan
