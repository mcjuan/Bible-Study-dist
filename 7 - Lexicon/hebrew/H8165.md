# H8165 שֵׂעִיר / שעיר

## Sêʻîyr

_(say-eer' | say-EER | say-EER)_

### Definition

hairy, shaggy; formed like H8163; rough; Seir, a mountain of Idumaea and its indigenous occupants, also one in Palestine; Seir; n-pr

- (n pr m) patriarch of the Horites, the inhabitants of Edom before the descendants of Esau, the Edomites
- (n pr terr) the land of Edom, south of the Dead Sea
- (n pr mont) a mountain range in Edom extending from the Dead Sea to the Elanitic Gulf
  - apparently also called 'Mount Seir' and extending most of the distance of the mountain range itself
- a mountain in northern Judah lying westward from Kirjath-jearim
