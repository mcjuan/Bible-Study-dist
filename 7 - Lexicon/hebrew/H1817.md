# H1817 דֶּלֶת / דלת

## deleth

_(deh'-leth | deh-LET | deh-LET)_

### Definition

from H1802; something swinging, i.e., the valve of a door; door (two-leaved), gate, leaf, lid; n-f

- door, gate
  - a door
  - a gate
  - (figuratively)
    - of chest lid
    - of crocodile jaws
    - of doors of the heavens
    - of an easily-accessible woman

## Note

See Psalm 141:3.
