# H5284 נַעֲמָתִי / נעמתי

## Naʻămâthîy

_(nah-am-aw-thee' | na-uh-maw-TEE | na-uh-ma-TEE)_

### Definition

pleasantness; patrial from a place corresponding in name (but not identical) with H5279; a Naamathite, or inhabitant of Naamah; Naamathite; a

- an inhabitant of Naamah (site unknown); describes Zophar the friend of Job
