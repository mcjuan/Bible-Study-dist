# H7973 שֶׁלַח / שלח

## shelach

_(sheh'-lakh | sheh-LA | sheh-LAHK)_

### Definition

from H7971; a missile of attack, i.e., spear; also (figuratively) a shoot of growth; i.e., branch; dart, plant, × put off, sword, weapon; n-m

- weapon, missile, sprout
  - missile, weapon
  - sprout, shoot
