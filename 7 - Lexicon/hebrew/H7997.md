# H7997 שָׁלַל / שלל

## shâlal

_(shaw-lal' | shaw-LAHL | sha-LAHL)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to drop or strip; by implication, to plunder; let fall, make self a prey, × of purpose, (make a, (take)) spoil; v

- (Qal) to draw out
- to spoil, plunder, take spoil
  - (Qal) to spoil, plunder
  - (Hithpolel) to be spoiled, be plundered
