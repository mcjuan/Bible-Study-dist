# H5774 עוּף / עוף

## ʻûwph

_(oof | oop | oof)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to cover (with wings or obscurity); hence (as denominative from H5775); to fly; also (by implication of dimness) to faint (from the darkness of swooning); brandish, be (wax) faint, flee away, fly (away), × set, shine forth, weary; v

- (v) to fly, fly about, fly away
  - (Qal)
    - to fly, hover
    - to fly away
  - (Hiphil) to cause to fly, light upon
  - (Polel)
    - to fly about or to and fro
    - to cause to fly to and fro, brandish
  - (Hithpolel) to fly away
- (Qal) to cover, be dark
- (n f) gloom
