# H7551 רָקַם / רקם

## râqam

_(raw-kam' | raw-KAHM | ra-KAHM)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to variegate color, i.e., embroider; by implication, to fabricate; embroiderer, needlework, curiously work; v

- to variegate, mix colors
  - (Qal) variegator (worker in colors) (participle)
  - (Pual) to be skilfully wrought or woven
