# H5700 עֶגְלוֹן / עגלון

## ʻEglôwn

_(eg-lawn' | eɡ-LONE | eɡ-LONE)_

### Definition

calf-like; from H5695; vituline; Eglon, the name of a place in Palestine and of a Moabitish king; Eglon; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) king of Moab who oppressed the children of Israel for 18 years before he was killed by the judge Ehud
- (n pr loc) a royal city in Canaan that opposed the conquest; located in the low lands of Judah
