# H7392 רָכַב / רכב

## râkab

_(raw-kab' | raw-KAHB | ra-HAHV)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to ride (on an animal or in a vehicle); causatively, to place upon (for riding or generally), to despatch; bring (on (horse-)back), carry, get (oneself) up, on (horse-)back, put, (cause to, make to) ride (in a chariot, on, -r), set; v

- to mount and ride, ride
  - (Qal)
    - to mount, mount and sit or ride
    - to ride, be riding
    - rider (subst)
  - (Hiphil)
    - to cause to ride, cause to (mount and) ride
    - to cause to draw (plough, etc)
    - to cause to ride upon (figuratively)
