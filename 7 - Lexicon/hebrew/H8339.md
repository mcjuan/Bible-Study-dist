# H8339 שֵׁשְׁבַּצַּר / ששבצר

## Shêshᵉbatstsar

_(shaysh-bats-tsar' | shay-sheb-bahts-SAHR | shay-sheh-ba-TSAHR)_

### Definition

worshipper of fire; of foreign derivation; Sheshbatstsar, Zerubbabel's Persian name; Sheshbazzar; n-pr-m

- the prince of Judah at the first return from exile in Babylon; usually identified as the Babylonian name for Zerubbabel
