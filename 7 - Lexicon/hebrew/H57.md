# H57 אָבֵל / אבל

## ʼâbêl

_(aw-bale' | aw-BALE | ah-VALE)_

### Definition

from H0056; lamenting; mourn(-er, -ing); a

- mourning
  - for the dead
  - because of calamity
  - of rites of mourning
- mourner (subst.)
  - for the dead
  - for calamity
