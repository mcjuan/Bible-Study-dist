# H8443 תּוֹעָפָה / תועפה

## tôwʻâphâh

_(to-aw-faw' | toh-aw-PAW | toh-ah-FA)_

### Definition

from H3286; (only in plural collective) weariness, i.e., (by implication) toil (treasure so obtained) or speed; plenty, strength; n-f

- eminence, lofty horns, summit
  - eminence (of towering horns, peaks, silver)
