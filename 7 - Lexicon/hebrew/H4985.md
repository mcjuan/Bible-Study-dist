# H4985 מָתַק / מתק

## mâthaq

_(maw-thak' | maw-TAHK | ma-TAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to suck, by implication, to relish, or (intransitively) be sweet; be (made, × take) sweet; v

- to be or become sweet or pleasing
  - (Qal)
    - to be or become sweet
    - to be or become pleasant
    - to suck
  - (Hiphil) to give sweet taste
