# H5557 סָלַף / סלף

## çâlaph

_(saw-laf' | saw-LAHP | sa-LAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to wrench, i.e., (figuratively) to subvert; overthrow, pervert; v

- to twist, pervert, distort, overturn, ruin
  - (Piel)
    - to pervert (of bribery)
    - to subvert, turn upside down, ruin
