# H8622 תְּקוּפָה / תקופה

## tᵉqûwphâh

_(tek-oo-faw' | teh-koo-PAW | teh-koo-FA)_

### Definition

or תְּקֻפָה; from H5362; a revolution, i.e., (of the sun) course, (of time) lapse; circuit, come about, end; n-f

- coming round, circuit of time or space, a turning, circuit
  - at the circuit (as adverb)
