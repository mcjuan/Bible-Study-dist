# H3701 כֶּסֶף / כסף

## keçeph

_(keh'-sef | keh-SEP | keh-SEF)_

### Definition

from H3700; silver (from its pale color); by implication, money; money, price, silver(-ling); n-m

- silver, money
  - silver
    - as metal
    - as ornament
    - as color
  - money, shekels, talents
