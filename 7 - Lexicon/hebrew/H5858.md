# H5858 עֵיבָל / עיבל

## ʻÊybâl

_(ay-bawl' | ay-BAWL | ay-VAHL)_

### Definition

stone, bare mountain; perhaps from an unused root probably meaning to be bald; bare; Ebal, a mountain of Palestine; Ebal; n-pr-m n-pr-loc

- (n pr m) a son of Shobal
- (n pr loc) mountain of cursing, north of Shechem and opposite Mount Gerizim
