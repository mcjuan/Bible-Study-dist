# H5159 נַחֲלָה / נחלה

## nachălâh

_(nakh-al-aw' | na-huh-LAW | na-huh-LA)_

### Definition

from H5157 (in its usual sense); properly, something inherited, i.e., (abstractly) occupancy, or (concretely) an heirloom; generally an estate, patrimony or portion; heritage, to inherit, inheritance, possession; n-f

- possession, property, inheritance, heritage
  - property
  - portion, share
  - inheritance, portion

## Note

Compare H5158.
