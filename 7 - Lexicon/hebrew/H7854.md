# H7854 שָׂטָן / שטן

## sâṭân

_(saw-tawn' | saw-TAWN | sa-TAHN)_

### Definition

from H7853; an opponent; especially (with the article prefixed) Satan, the arch-enemy of good; adversary, Satan, withstand; n-m

- adversary, one who withstands
  - adversary (in general—personal or national)
- superhuman adversary
  - Satan (as noun pr)
