# H6083 עָפָר / עפר

## ʻâphâr

_(aw-fawr' | aw-PAWR | ah-FAHR)_

### Definition

from H6080; dust (as powdered or gray); hence, clay, earth, mud; ashes, dust, earth, ground, morter, powder, rubbish; n-m

- dry earth, dust, powder, ashes, earth, ground, mortar, rubbish
  - dry or loose earth
  - debris
  - mortar
  - ore
