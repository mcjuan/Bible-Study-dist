# H5197 נָטַף / נטף

## nâṭaph

_(naw-taf' | naw-TAHP | na-TAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to ooze, i.e., distil gradually; by implication, to fall in drops; figuratively, to speak by inspiration; drop(-ping), prophesy(-et); v

- to drop, drip, distil, prophesy, preach, discourse
  - (Qal) to drop, drip
  - (Hiphil)
    - to drip
    - to drop (prophecy)
