# H6288 פְּאֹרָה / פארה

## pᵉʼôrâh

_(peh-o-raw' | peh-oh-RAW | peh-oh-RA)_

### Definition

or פֹּרָאה; or פֻּארָה; from H6286; properly, ornamentation, i.e., (plural) foliage (including the limbs) as bright green; bough, branch, sprig; n-f

- bough, branch, shoot
