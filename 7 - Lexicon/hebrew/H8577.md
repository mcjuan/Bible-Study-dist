# H8577 תַּנִּין / תנין

## tannîyn

_(tan-neen' | tahn-NEEN | ta-NEEN)_

### Definition

or תַּנִּים; (Ezekiel 29:3), intensive from the same as H8565; a marine or land monster, i.e., sea-serpent or jackal; dragon, sea-monster, serpent, whale; n-m

- dragon, serpent, sea monster
  - dragon or dinosaur
  - sea or river monster
  - serpent, venomous snake
