# H4570 מַעְגָּל / מעגל

## maʻgâl

_(mah-gawl' | ma-ɡAWL | ma-ɡAHL)_

### Definition

or feminine מַעְגָּלָה; from the same as H5696; a track (literally or figuratively); also a rampart (as circular); going, path, trench, way(-side); n-m

- entrenchment, track
  - circumvallation, entrenchment
  - track
