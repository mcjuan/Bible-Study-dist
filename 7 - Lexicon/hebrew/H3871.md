# H3871 לוּחַ / לוח

## lûwach

_(loo'-akh | LOO-ah | LOO-ak)_

### Definition

or לֻחַ; from a primitive root; probably meaning to glisten; a tablet (as polished), of stone, wood or metal; board, plate, table; n-m

- board, slab, tablet, plank
  - tablets (of stone)
  - boards (of wood)
  - plate (of metal)
