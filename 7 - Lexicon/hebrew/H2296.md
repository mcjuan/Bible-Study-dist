# H2296 חָגַר / חגר

## châgar

_(khaw-gar' | haw-ɡAHR | ha-ɡAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to gird on (as a belt, armor, etc.); be able to put on, be afraid, appointed, gird, restrain, × on every side; v

- to gird, gird on, gird oneself, put on a belt
  - (Qal)
    - to gird
    - to gird on, bind on
    - to gird oneself
