# H6475 פָּצָה / פצה

## pâtsâh

_(paw-tsaw' | paw-TSAW | pa-TSA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to rend, i.e., open (especially the mouth); deliver, gape, open, rid, utter; v

- to part, open, separate, set free
  - (Qal)
    - to open (mouth), utter
    - to snatch away, set free
