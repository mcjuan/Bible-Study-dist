# H5190 נָטַל / נטל

## nâṭal

_(naw-tal' | naw-TAHL | na-TAHL)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to lift; by implication, to impose; bear, offer, take up; v

- to lift, bear, bear up
  - (Qal) to lift, lift over, lift upon, set up
  - (Piel) to bear up
