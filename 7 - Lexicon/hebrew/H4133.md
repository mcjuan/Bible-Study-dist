# H4133 מוֹטָה / מוטה

## môwṭâh

_(mo-taw' | moh-TAW | moh-TA)_

### Definition

feminine of H4132; a pole; by implication, an ox-bow; hence, a yoke (either literal or figurative); bands, heavy, staves, yoke; n-f

- pole, bar of yoke
  - pole
  - bar of yoke
    - of oppression (figuratively)
