# H4586 מְעוּנִי / מעוני

## Mᵉʻûwnîy

_(meh-oo-nee' | meh-oo-NEE | meh-oo-NEE)_

### Definition

habitations; or מְעִינִי; probably patrial from H4584; a Meunite, or inhabitant of Maon (only in plural); Mehunim(-s), Meunim; n-pr-m
