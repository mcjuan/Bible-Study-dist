# H8032 שִׁלְשׁוֹם / שלשום

## shilshôwm

_(shil-shome' | shil-SHOME | sheel-SHOME)_

### Definition

or שִׁלְשֹׁם; from the same as H8028; trebly, i.e., (in time) day before yesterday; + before (that time, -time), excellent things (from the margin), + heretofore, three days, + time past; adv

- day before yesterday, three days ago
  - an idiom for 'heretofore in times past'
