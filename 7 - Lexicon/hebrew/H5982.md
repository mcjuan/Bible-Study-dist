# H5982 עַמּוּד / עמוד

## ʻammûwd

_(am-mood' | am-MOOD | ah-MOOD)_

### Definition

or עַמֻּד; from H5975; a column (as standing); also a stand, i.e., platform; × apiece, pillar; n-m

- pillar, column
  - pillar
  - column, upright
  - column (of smoke)
