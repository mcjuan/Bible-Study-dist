# H8425 תּוֹגַרְמָה / תוגרמה

## Tôwgarmâh

_(to-gar-maw' | toh-ɡahr-MAW | toh-ɡahr-MA)_

### Definition

thou will break her; or תֹּגַרְמָה; probably of foreign derivation; Togarmah, a son of Gomer and his posterity; Togarmah; n-pr

- (n pr m) son of Gomer, grandson of Japheth, and great grandson of Noah
- (n pr terr) territory settled by the descendants of Togarmah
  - probably the area known as Armenia
