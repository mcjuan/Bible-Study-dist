# H5483 סוּס / סוס

## çûwç

_(soos | soos | soos)_

### Definition

or סֻס; from an unused root meaning to skip (properly, for joy); a horse (as leaping); crane, horse(-back, -hoof); n-m

- swallow, swift
- horse
  - chariot horses

## Note

Compare H6571.
