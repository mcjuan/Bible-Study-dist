# H3713 כְּפוֹר / כפור

## kᵉphôwr

_(kef-ore' | keh-PORE | keh-FORE)_

### Definition

from H3722; properly, a cover, i.e., (by implication) a tankard (or covered goblet); also white frost (as covering the ground); bason, hoar(-y) frost; n-m

- bowl, basin
- hoar frost, frost
