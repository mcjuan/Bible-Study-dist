# H5631 סָרִיס / סריס

## çârîyç

_(saw-reece' | saw-REES | sa-REES)_

### Definition

or סָרִס; from an unused root meaning to castrate; a eunuch; by implication, valet (especially of the female apartments), and thus, a minister of state; chamberlain, eunuch, officer; n-m

- official, eunuch

## Note

Compare H7249.
