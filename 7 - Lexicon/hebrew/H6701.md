# H6701 צוּרִישַׁדַּי / צורישדי

## Tsûwrîyshadday

_(tsoo-ree-shad-dah'-ee | tsoo-ree-shahd-DAI | tsoo-ree-sha-DAI)_

### Definition

my rock is almighty; from H6697 and H7706; rock of (the) Almighty; Tsurishaddai, an Israelite; Zurishaddai; n-pr-m

- father of Shelumiel, the chief of the Simeonites at the time of the exodus
