# H5216 נִיר / ניר

## nîyr

_(neer | neer | neer)_

### Definition

or נִר; also נֵיר; or נֵר; or (feminine) נֵרָה; from a primitive root (see H5214; H5135) properly, meaning to glisten; a lamp (i.e., the burner) or light (literally or figuratively); candle, lamp, light; n-m

- lamp
