# H8336 שֵׁשׁ / שש

## shêsh

_(shaysh | shaysh | shaysh)_

### Definition

or (for alliteration with H4897) שְׁשִׁי; for H7893; bleached stuff, i.e., white linen or (by analogy) marble; × blue, fine (twined) linen, marble, silk; n-m

- something bleached white, byssus, linen, fine linen
- alabaster, similar stone, marble
