# H5895 עַיִר / עיר

## ʻayir

_(ah'-yeer | ah-YIR | ah-YEER)_

### Definition

from H5782 in the sense of raising (i.e., bearing a burden); properly, a young ass (as just broken to a load); hence an ass-colt; (ass) colt, foal, young ass; n-m

- he-ass, male ass
