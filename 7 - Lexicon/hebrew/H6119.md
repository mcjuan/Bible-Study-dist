# H6119 עָקֵב / עקב

## ʻâqêb

_(aw-kabe' | aw-KABE | ah-KAVE)_

### Definition

or (feminine) עִקְּבָה; from H6117; a heel (as protuberant); hence, a track; figuratively, the rear (of an army); heel, (horse-)hoof, last, lier in wait (by mistake for H6120), (foot-)step; n-m

- heel, rear, footprint, hinder part, hoof, rear of a troop, footstep
  - heel
  - mark of heel, footprint
  - hinder part, rear
