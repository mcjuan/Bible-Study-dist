# H7304 רָוַח / רוח

## râvach

_(raw-vakh' | raw-WA | ra-VAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root (identical with H7306); properly, to breathe freely, i.e., revive; by implication, to have ample room; be refreshed, large; v

- to be wide, be spacious, breathe
  - (Qal) to breathe easily, be relieved
  - (Pual) spacious (participle)
