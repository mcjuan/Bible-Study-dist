# H5748 עוּגָב / עוגב

## ʻûwgâb

_(oo-gawb' | oo-ɡAWB | oo-ɡAHV)_

### Definition

or עֻגָּב; from H5689 in the original sense of breathing; a reed-instrument of music; organ; n-m

- a musical instrument
  - perhaps a flute, reed-pipe, or panpipes
