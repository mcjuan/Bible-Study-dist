# H6828 צָפוֹן / צפון

## tsâphôwn

_(tsaw-fone' | tsaw-PONE | tsa-FONE)_

### Definition

or צָפֹן; from H6845; properly, hidden, i.e., dark; used only of the north as a quarter (gloomy and unknown); north(-ern, -ward) (side, wind); n-f

- north (of direction), northward
  - north
  - northward
