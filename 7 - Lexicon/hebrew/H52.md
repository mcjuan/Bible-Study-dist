# H52 אֲבִישַׁי / אבישי

## ʼĂbîyshay

_(ab-ee-shah'ee | uh-bee-SHAI | uh-vee-SHAI)_

### Definition

my father is Jesse, my father is a gift; or (shorter) אַבְשַׁי ; from H0001 and H7862; father of a gift (i.e., probably generous); Abishai, an Israelite; Abishai; n-pr-m

- grandson of Jesse, nephew of David via his sister Zeruiah, brother of Joab
