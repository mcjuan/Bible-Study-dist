# H7508 רְפִידִים / רפידים

## Rᵉphîydîym

_(ref-ee-deem' | reh-pee-DEEM | reh-fee-DEEM)_

### Definition

rests, stays, resting places; plural of the masculine of the same as H7507; ballusters; Rephidim, a place in the Desert; Rephidim; n-pr-m

- a station of Israel in the wilderness between Egypt and Sinai
