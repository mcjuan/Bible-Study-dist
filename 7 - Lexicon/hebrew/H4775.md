# H4775 מָרַד / מרד

## mârad

_(maw-rad' | maw-RAHD | ma-RAHD)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to rebel; rebel(-lious); v

- to rebel, revolt, be rebellious
  - (Qal) to rebel, revolt
    - against human king
    - against God
    - against light (poetic)
