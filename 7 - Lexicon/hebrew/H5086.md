# H5086 נָדַף / נדף

## nâdaph

_(naw-daf' | naw-DAHP | na-DAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to shove asunder, i.e., disperse; drive (away, to and fro), thrust down, shaken, tossed to and fro; v

- to drive, drive away, drive asunder
  - (Qal) to drive about
  - (Niphal) to be driven, be driven about
