# H783 אַרְתַּחְשַׁשְׁתָּא / ארתחששתא

## ʼArtachshashtâʼ

_(ar-takh-shash-taw' | ar-ta-shahsh-TAW | ar-tahk-shahsh-TA)_

### Definition

I will make the spoiled to boil, I will stir myself (in) winter; or אַרְתַּחְשַׁשְׁתְּא; or by permutation אַרְתַּחְשַׁסְתְּא; of foreign origin; Artachshasta (or Artaxerxes), a title (rather than name) of several Persian kings; Artaxerxes; n-pr-m

- son and successor of Xerxes as emperor of Persia, 465–424 BC
