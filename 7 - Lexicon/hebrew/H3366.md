# H3366 יְקָר / יקר

## yᵉqâr

_(yek-awr' | yeh-KAWR | yeh-KAHR)_

### Definition

from H3365; value, i.e., (concretely) wealth; abstractly, costliness, dignity; honour, precious (things), price; n-m

- price, value, preciousness, honor, splendor, pomp
  - preciousness
  - price
  - honor, esteem
