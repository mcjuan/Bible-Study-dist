# H5648 עֲבַד / עבד

## ʻăbad

_(ab-bad' | uh-BAHD | uh-VAHD)_

### Definition

corresponding to H5647; to do, make, prepare, keep, etc; × cut, do, execute, go on, make, move, work; v

- to make, do
  - (P'al)
    - to make, create
    - to do, perform
  - (Ithp'al)
    - to be made into
    - to be done, be wrought, be performed, be executed, be carried out
