# H7951 שָׁלָה / שלה

## shâlâh

_(shaw-law' | shaw-LAW | sha-LA)_

### Definition

or שָׁלַו; (Job 3:26), a primitive root; to be tranquil, i.e., secure or successful; be happy, prosper, be in safety; v

- to be at rest, prosper, be quiet, be at ease
  - (Qal)
    - to be or have quiet
    - to be at ease, prosper
