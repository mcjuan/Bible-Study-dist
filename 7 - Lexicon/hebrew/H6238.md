# H6238 עָשַׁר / עשר

## ʻâshar

_(aw-shar' | aw-SHAHR | ah-SHAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to accumulate; chiefly (specifically) to grow (causatively, make) rich; be(-come) (make, make self, waxen) rich, make (1 Kings 22:48 in the margin); v

- to be or become rich or wealthy, enrich, pretend to be rich
  - (Qal) to be or become rich
  - (Hiphil)
    - to make rich
    - to gain riches
  - (Hithpael) to enrich oneself, pretend to be rich
