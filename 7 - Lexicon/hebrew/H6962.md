# H6962 קוּט / קוט

## qûwṭ

_(koot | koot | koot)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to cut off, i.e., (figuratively) detest; begrieved, loathe self; v

- to loathe, be grieved, feel a loathing
  - (Qal) to feel a loathing
  - (Niphal)
    - to feel loathing against self
    - to detest
  - (Hithpolel) to loathe oneself
