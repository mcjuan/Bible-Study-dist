# H328 אַט / אט

## ʼaṭ

_(at | at | at)_

### Definition

from an unused root perhaps meaning to move softly; (as an adverb) gently; charmer; adv

- gentleness, softness
  - gently
  - flowing
  - softly
  - enchanter, necromancer (substantive)
