# H5127 נוּס / נוס

## nûwç

_(noos | noos | noos)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to flit, i.e., vanish away (subside, escape; causatively, chase, impel, deliver); × abate, away, be displayed, (make to) flee (away, -ing), put to flight, × hide, lift up a standard; v

- to flee, escape
  - (Qal)
    - to flee
    - to escape
    - to take flight, depart, disappear
    - to fly (to the attack) on horseback
  - (Polel) to drive at
  - (Hithpolel) to take flight
  - (Hiphil)
    - to put to flight
    - to drive hastily
    - to cause to disappear, hide
