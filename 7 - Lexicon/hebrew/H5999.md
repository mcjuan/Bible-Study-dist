# H5999 עָמָל / עמל

## ʻâmâl

_(aw-mawl' | aw-MAWL | ah-MAHL)_

### Definition

from H5998; toil, i.e., wearing effort; hence, worry, whether of body or mind; grievance(-vousness), iniquity, labour, mischief, miserable(-sery), pain(-ful), perverseness, sorrow, toil, travail, trouble, wearisome, wickedness; n

- toil, trouble, labor
  - trouble
  - trouble, mischief
  - toil, labor
