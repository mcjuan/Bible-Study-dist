# H5081 נָדִיב / נדיב

## nâdîyb

_(naw-deeb' | naw-DEEB | na-DEEV)_

### Definition

from H5068; properly, voluntary, i.e., generous; hence, magnanimous; as noun, a grandee (sometimes a tyrant); free, liberal (things), noble, prince, willing (hearted); a

- (adj) inclined, willing, noble, generous
  - incited, inclined, willing
  - noble, princely (in rank)
  - noble (in mind and character)
- (n m) noble one
