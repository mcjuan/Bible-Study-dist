# H7337 רָחַב / רחב

## râchab

_(raw-khab' | raw-HAHB | ra-HAHV)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to broaden (intransitive or transitive, literal or figurative); be an en-(make) large(-ing), make room, make (open) wide; v

- to be or grow wide, be or grow large
  - (Qal) to be widened, be enlarged
  - (Niphal) broad or roomy pasture (participle)
  - (Hiphil)
    - to make large
    - to enlarge
