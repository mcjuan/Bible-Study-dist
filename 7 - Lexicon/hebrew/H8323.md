# H8323 שָׂרַר / שרר

## sârar

_(saw-rar' | saw-RAHR | sa-RAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to have (transitively, exercise; reflexively, get) dominion; × altogether, make self a prince, (bear) rule; v

- to be or act as prince, rule, contend, have power, prevail over, reign, govern
  - (Qal) to rule over, govern
  - (Hithpael) to lord it over
