# H7617 שָׁבָה / שבה

## shâbâh

_(shaw-baw' | shaw-BAW | sha-VA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to transport into captivity; (bring away, carry, carry away, lead, lead away, take) captive(-s), drive (take) away; v

- to take captive
  - (Qal)
    - to take captive
    - captive (participle)
    - to lead captive
  - (Niphal) to be taken captive
