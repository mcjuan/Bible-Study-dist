# H1198 בַּעַר / בער

## baʻar

_(bah'-ar | ba-AR | ba-AR)_

### Definition

from H1197; properly, foot (as consumed); i.e., (by exten.) of cattle brutishness; (concretely) stupid; brutish (person), foolish; n-m

- brutishness, stupidity, brutish (person)
