# H5383 נָשָׁה / נשה

## nâshâh

_(naw-shaw' | naw-SHAW | na-SHA)_

### Definition

a primitive root (rather identical with H5382, in the sense of H5378); to lend or (by reciprocity) borrow on security or interest; creditor, exact, extortioner, lend, usurer, lend on (taker on) usury; v

- to lend, be a creditor
  - (Qal)
    - to lend
    - creditor (participle) (subst)
  - (Hiphil) to lend
