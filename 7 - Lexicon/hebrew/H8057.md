# H8057 שִׂמְחָה / שמחה

## simchâh

_(sim-khaw' | sim-HAW | seem-HA)_

### Definition

from H8056; blithesomeness or glee, (religious or festival); × exceeding(-ly), gladness, joy(-fulness), mirth, pleasure, rejoice(-ing); n-f

- joy, mirth, gladness
  - mirth, gladness, joy, gaiety, pleasure
  - joy (of God)
  - glad result, happy issue
