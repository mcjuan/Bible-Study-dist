# H8548 תָּמִיד / תמיד

## tâmîyd

_(taw-meed' | taw-MEED | ta-MEED)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to stretch; properly, continuance (as indefinite extension); but used only (attributively as adjective) constant (or adverbially, constantly); elliptically the regular (daily) sacrifice; alway(-s), continual (employment, -ly), daily, (n-)ever(-more), perpetual; n-m

- continuity, perpetuity, to stretch
  - continually, continuously (as adverb)
  - continuity (subst)
