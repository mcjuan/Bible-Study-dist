# H854 אֵת / את

## ʼêth

_(ayth | ate | ate)_

### Definition

probably from H0579; properly, nearness (used only as a preposition or an adverb), near; hence, generally, with, by, at, among, etc; against, among, before, by, for, from, in(-to), (out) of, with; prep

- with, near, together with
  - with, together with
  - with (of relationship)
  - near (of place)
  - with (poss.)
  - from...with, from (with other prep)

## Note

Often with another prepositional prefix.
