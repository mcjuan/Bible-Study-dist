# H8615 תִּקְוָה / תקוה

## tiqvâh

_(tik-vaw' | tik-WAW | teek-VA)_

### Definition

from H6960; (compare H6961); literally a cord (as an attachment); figuratively, expectancy; expectation(-ted), hope, live, thing that I long for; n-f

- cord
- hope, expectation
  - hope
  - ground of hope
  - things hoped for, outcome
