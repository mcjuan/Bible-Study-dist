# H6938 קֵדָר / קדר

## Qêdâr

_(kay-dawr' | kay-DAWR | kay-DAHR)_

### Definition

dark; from H6937; dusky (of the skin or the tent); Kedar, a son of Ishmael; also (collectively) Bedouin (as his descendants or representatives); Kedar; n-pr

- (n pr m) a son of Ishmael
- (n pr people) the descendants of Kedar
