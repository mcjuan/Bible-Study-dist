# H8398 תֵּבֵל / תבל

## têbêl

_(tay-bale' | tay-BALE | tay-VALE)_

### Definition

from H2986; the earth (as moist and therefore inhabited); by extension, the globe; by implication, its inhabitants; specifically, a particular land, as Babylonia, Palestine; habitable part, world; n-f

- world
