# H7515 רָפַשׂ / רפש

## râphas

_(raw-fas' | raw-PAHS | ra-FAHS)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to trample, i.e., roil water; foul, trouble; v

- to stamp, tread, foul by stamping or treading
  - (Qal) to tread down, foul by treading, foul with the feet
  - (Niphal) to be fouled
