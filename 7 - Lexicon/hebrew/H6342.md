# H6342 פָּחַד / פחד

## pâchad

_(paw-kkad' | paw-HAHD | pa-HAHD)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to be startled (by a sudden alarm); hence, to fear in general; be afraid, stand in awe, (be in) fear, make to shake; v

- to fear, tremble, revere, dread, be in awe or dread
  - (Qal)
    - to be in dread
    - to be in awe
  - (Piel) to be in great dread
  - (Hiphil) to cause to dread
