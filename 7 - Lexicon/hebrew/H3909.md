# H3909 לָט / לט

## lâṭ

_(lawt | lawt | laht)_

### Definition

a form of H3814 or else participle from H3874; properly, covered, i.e., secret; by implication, incantation; also secrecy or (adverb) covertly; enchantment, privily, secretly, softly; n-m

- secrecy, mystery, enchantment
  - secrecy, secretly
  - mystery, enchantment
