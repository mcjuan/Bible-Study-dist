# H8266 שָׁקַר / שקר

## shâqar

_(shaw-kar' | shaw-KAHR | sha-KAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to cheat, i.e., be untrue (usually in words); fail, deal falsely, lie; v

- to do or deal falsely, be false, trick, cheat
  - (Piel) to deal falsely
  - (Qal) to deal falsely
