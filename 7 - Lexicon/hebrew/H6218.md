# H6218 עָשׂוֹר / עשור

## ʻâsôwr

_(aw-sore' | aw-SORE | ah-SORE)_

### Definition

or עָשֹׂר; from H6235; ten; by abbreviated form ten strings, and so a decachord; (instrument of) ten (strings, -th); n-m

- ten, decade
  - ten, tenth
  - ten-stringed, harp
