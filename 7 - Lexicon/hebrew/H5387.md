# H5387 נָשִׂיא / נשיא

## nâsîyʼ

_(naw-see' | naw-SEE | na-SEE)_

### Definition

or נָשִׂא; from H5375; properly, an exalted one, i.e., a king or sheik; also a rising mist; captain, chief, cloud, governor, prince, ruler, vapour; n-m

- one lifted up, chief, prince, captain, leader
- rising mist, vapour
