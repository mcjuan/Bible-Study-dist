# H7626 שֵׁבֶט / שבט

## shêbeṭ

_(shay'-bet | shay-BET | shay-VET)_

### Definition

from an unused root probably meaning to branch off; a scion, i.e., (literally) a stick (for punishing, writing, fighting, ruling, walking, etc.) or (figuratively) a clan; × correction, dart, rod, sceptre, staff, tribe; n-m

- rod, staff, branch, offshoot, club, sceptre, tribe
  - rod, staff
  - shaft (of spear, dart)
  - club (of shepherd's implement)
  - truncheon, sceptre (mark of authority)
  - clan, tribe
