# H7161 קֶרֶן / קרן

## qeren

_(keh'-ren | keh-REN | keh-REN)_

### Definition

from H7160; a horn (as projecting); by implication, a flask, cornet; by resemblance. an elephant's tooth (i.e., ivory), a corner (of the altar), a peak (of a mountain), a ray (of light); figuratively, power; × hill, horn; n-f

- (n f) horn
  - horn
  - of strength (figuratively)
  - flask (container for oil)
  - horn (as musical instrument)
  - horn (of horn-like projections on the altar)
  - of rays of light
  - hill
- (n pr loc) a place conquered by Israel probably in Bashan
