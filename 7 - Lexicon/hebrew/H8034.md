# H8034 שֵׁם / שם

## shêm

_(shame | shame | shame)_

### Definition

a primitive word [perhaps rather from H7760 through the idea of definite and conspicuous position; compare H8064]; an appellation, as a mark or memorial of individuality; by implication honor, authority, character; + base, (in-)fame(-ous), named(-d), renown, report; n-m

- name
  - name
  - reputation, fame, glory
  - the Name (as designation of God)
  - memorial, monument
