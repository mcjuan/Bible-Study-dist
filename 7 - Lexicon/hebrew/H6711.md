# H6711 צָחַק / צחק

## tsâchaq

_(tsaw-khak' | tsaw-HAHK | tsa-HAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to laugh outright (in merriment or scorn); by implication, to sport; laugh, mock, play, make sport; v

- to laugh, mock, play
  - (Qal) to laugh
  - (Piel)
    - to jest
    - to sport, play, make sport, toy with, make a toy of
