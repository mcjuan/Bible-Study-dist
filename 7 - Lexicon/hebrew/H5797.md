# H5797 עֹז / עז

## ʻôz

_(oze | ohdz | oze)_

### Definition

or (fully) עוֹז; from H5810; strength in various applications (force, security, majesty, praise); boldness, loud, might, power, strength, strong; n-m

- might, strength
  - material or physical
  - personal or social or political
