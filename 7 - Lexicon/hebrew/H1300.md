# H1300 בָּרָק / ברק

## bârâq

_(baw-rawk' | baw-RAWK | ba-RAHK)_

### Definition

from H1299; lightning; by analogy, a gleam; concretely, a flashing sword; bright, glitter(-ing sword), lightning; n-m

- lightning
  - lightnings, lightning flashes
  - of flashing arrow-head (figuratively)
