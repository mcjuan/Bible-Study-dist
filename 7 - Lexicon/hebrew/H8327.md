# H8327 שָׁרַשׁ / שרש

## shârash

_(shaw-rash' | shaw-RAHSH | sha-RAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to root, i.e., strike into the soil, or (by implication) to pluck from it; (take, cause to take) root (out); v

- to uproot, take root, deal with the roots
  - (Piel) to root up, root out
  - (Pual) to be rooted up or out (of produce)
  - (Poel) to take root
  - (Poal) to take root
  - (Hiphil) to take root, cause to take root
