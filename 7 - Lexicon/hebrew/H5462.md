# H5462 סָגַר / סגר

## çâgar

_(saw-gar' | saw-ɡAHR | sa-ɡAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to shut up; figuratively, to surrender; close up, deliver (up), give over (up), inclose, × pure, repair, shut (in, self, out, up, up together), stop, × straitly; v

- to shut, close
  - (Qal)
    - to shut
    - to close, close up
    - closed up, closely joined, shut up
  - (Niphal)
    - to be shut up
    - to be shut or closed
  - (Piel) to shut up, deliver up
  - (Pual) to be shut up
  - (Hiphil)
    - to deliver up
    - to shut up, imprison
