# H7440 רִנָּה / רנה

## rinnâh

_(rin-naw' | rin-NAW | ree-NA)_

### Definition

from H7442; properly, a creaking (or shrill sound), i.e., shout (of joy or grief); cry, gladness, joy, proclamation, rejoicing, shouting, sing(-ing), triumph; n-f

- ringing cry
  - of entreaty, supplication
  - in proclamation, joy, praise
