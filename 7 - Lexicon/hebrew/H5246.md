# H5246 נָמֵר / נמר

## nâmêr

_(naw-mare' | naw-MARE | na-MARE)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning properly, to filtrate, i.e., be limpid (compare H5247 and H5249); and thus to spot or stain as if by dripping; a leopard (from its stripes); leopard; n-m

- leopard
