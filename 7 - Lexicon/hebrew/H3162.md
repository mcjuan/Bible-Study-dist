# H3162 יַחַד / יחד

## yachad

_(yakh'-ad | ya-HAHD | ya-HAHD)_

### Definition

from H3161; properly, a unit, i.e., (adverb) unitedly; alike, at all (once), both, likewise, only, (al-)together, withal; n-m

- (n m) union, unitedness
- (adv) together, altogether, all together, alike
