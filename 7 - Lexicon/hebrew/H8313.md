# H8313 שָׂרַף / שרף

## sâraph

_(saw-raf' | saw-RAHP | sa-RAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to be (causatively, set) on fire; (cause to, make a) burn(-ing, up) kindle, × utterly; v

- to burn
  - (Qal) to burn
  - (Niphal) to be burned
  - (Piel) burner, burning (participle)
  - (Pual) to be burnt up, be burned
