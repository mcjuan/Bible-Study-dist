# H4906 מַשְׂכִּית / משכית

## maskîyth

_(mas-keeth' | mahs-KEET | mahs-KEET)_

### Definition

from the same as H7906; a figure (carved on stone, the wall, or any object); figuratively, imagination; conceit, image(-ry), picture, × wish; n-f

- show-piece, figure, imagination, image, idol, picture
  - show-piece, carved figure (of idols)
  - imagination, conceit
