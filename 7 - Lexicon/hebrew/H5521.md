# H5521 סֻכָּה / סכה

## çukkâh

_(sook-kaw' | sook-KAW | soo-KA)_

### Definition

feminine of H5520; a hut or lair; booth, cottage, covert, pavilion, tabernacle, tent; n-f

- thicket, covert, booth
  - thicket
  - booth (rude or temporary shelter)
