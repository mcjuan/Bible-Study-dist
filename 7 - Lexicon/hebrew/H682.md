# H682 אָצֵל / אצל

## ʼÂtsêl

_(aw-tsale' | aw-TSALE | ah-TSALE)_

### Definition

proximity, he has reserved, reserved; from H0680; noble; Atsel, the name of a place in Palestine; Azal, Azel; n-pr-loc n-pr-m

- a place apparently near Jerusalem
- (n pr m) a Benjamite descendant of Saul and Jonathan
