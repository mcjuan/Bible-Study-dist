# H8289 שָׁרוֹן / שרון

## Shârôwn

_(shaw-rone' | shaw-RONE | sha-RONE)_

### Definition

a plain; probably abridged from H3474; plain; Sharon, the name of a place in Palestine; Lasharon, Sharon; n-pr

- (n m) plain, level
- (n pr loc) the district lying between the mountains of central Palestine and the Mediterranean Sea and north of Joppa
- a district on the east of the Jordan around Gilead and Bashan
