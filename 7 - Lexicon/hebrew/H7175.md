# H7175 קֶרֶשׁ / קרש

## qeresh

_(keh'-resh | keh-RESH | keh-RESH)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to split off; a slab or plank; by implication, a deck of a ship; bench, board; n-m

- board, boards, plank
  - board
  - boards
