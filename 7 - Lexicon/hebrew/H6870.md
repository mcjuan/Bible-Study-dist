# H6870 צְרוּיָה / צרויה

## Tsᵉrûwyâh

_(tser-oo-yaw' | tseh-roo-YAW | tseh-roo-YA)_

### Definition

balsam; feminine passive participle from the same as H6875; wounded; Tserujah, an Israelitess; Zeruiah; n-pr-f

- sister of David and mother of the 3 leading heroes of David's army—Abishai, Joab, and Asahel
