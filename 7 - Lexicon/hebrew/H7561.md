# H7561 רָשַׁע / רשע

## râshaʻ

_(raw-shah' | raw-SHA | ra-SHA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to be (causatively, do or declare) wrong; by implication, to disturb, violate; condemn, make trouble, vex, be (commit, deal, depart, do) wicked(-ly, -ness); v

- to be wicked, act wickedly
  - (Qal)
    - to be wicked, act wickedly
    - to be guilty, be condemned
  - (Hiphil)
    - to condemn as guilty (in civil relations)
    - to condemn as guilty (in ethical or religious relations)
    - to act wickedly (in ethics and religion)
