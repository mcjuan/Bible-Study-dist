# H2962 טֶרֶם / טרם

## ṭerem

_(teh'-rem | teh-REM | teh-REM)_

### Definition

from an unused root apparently meaning to interrupt or suspend; properly, non-occurrence; used adverbially, not yet or before; before, ere, not yet; prep

- before, not yet, before that
