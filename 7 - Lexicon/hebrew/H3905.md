# H3905 לָחַץ / לחץ

## lâchats

_(law-khats' | law-HAHTS | la-HAHTS)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to press, i.e., (figuratively) to distress; afflict, crush, force, hold fast, oppress(-or), thrust self; v

- to squeeze, press, oppress
  - (Qal)
    - to squeeze, press
    - to oppress
  - (Niphal) to squeeze oneself
