# H2251 חָבַט / חבט

## châbaṭ

_(khaw-bat' | haw-BAHT | ha-VAHT)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to knock out or off; beat (off, out), thresh; v

- to beat, beat out, beat off, thresh
  - (Qal)
    - to beat out
    - to beat off
  - (Niphal) to be beaten out
