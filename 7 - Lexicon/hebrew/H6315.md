# H6315 פּוּחַ / פוח

## pûwach

_(poo'akh | POO-ah | POO-ak)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to puff, i.e., blow with the breath or air; hence, to fan (as a breeze), to utter, to kindle (a fire), to scoff; blow (upon), break, puff, bring into a snare, speak, utter; v

- to breathe, blow
  - (Qal) to breathe
  - (Hiphil)
    - to cause to exhale or breathe
    - to puff, snort
    - to excite, inflame
    - to puff, pant for it
    - to breathe out, utter
    - to blow, blast
