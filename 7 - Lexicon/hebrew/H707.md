# H707 אָרַג / ארג

## ʼârag

_(aw-rag' | aw-RAHɡ | ah-RAHɡ)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to plait or weave; weaver(-r); v

- to weave
  - (Qal)
    - to weave (cloth)
    - to braid (Samson's hair)
    - weaver (subst.)
    - intrigue (figuratively)
