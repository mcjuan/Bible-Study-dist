# H8184 שְׂעֹרָה / שערה

## sᵉʻôrâh

_(seh-o-raw' | seh-oh-RAW | seh-oh-RA)_

### Definition

or שְׂעוֹרָה (feminine meaning the plant); and (masculine meaning the grain); also שְׂעֹר; or שְׂעוֹר; from H8175 in the sense of roughness; barley (as villose); barley; n-f

- barley
  - barley (of the plant)
  - barley (of the meal or grain)
