# H7922 שֶׂכֶל / שכל

## sekel

_(seh'-kel | seh-KEL | seh-HEL)_

### Definition

or שֵׂכֶל; from H7919; intelligence; by implication, success; discretion, knowledge, policy, prudence, sense, understanding, wisdom, wise; n-m

- prudence, insight, understanding
  - prudence, good sense
  - insight, understanding
  - cunning, craft (bad sense)
