# H887 בָּאַשׁ / באש

## bâʼash

_(baw-ash' | baw-ASH | ba-ASH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to smell bad; figuratively, to be offensive morally; (make to) be abhorred (had in abomination, loathsome, odious), (cause a, make to) stink(-ing savour), × utterly; v

- to have a bad smell, stink, smell bad
  - (Qal) to stink, smell bad
  - (Niphal)
    - to become odious
    - to make oneself odious
  - (Hiphil)
    - to stink, emit a stinking odor
    - to cause to stink
    - of wickedness (figuratively)
  - (Hithpael) to make oneself odious
- to become odious (i.e., abhorred)
- to be evil, to act wickedly
