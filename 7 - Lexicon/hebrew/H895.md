# H895 בַּבֶל / בבל

## Babel

_(baw-bel' | ba-BEL | ba-VEL)_

### Definition

confusion (by mixing); corresponding to H0894; Babel (i.e., Babylon), including Babylonia and the Babylonian empire; Babylon; n-pr-loc

- Babel or Babylon, the ancient site and/or capital of Babylonia (modern Hillah) situated on the Euphrates
