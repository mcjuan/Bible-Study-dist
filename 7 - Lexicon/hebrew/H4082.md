# H4082 מְדִינָה / מדינה

## mᵉdîynâh

_(med-ee-naw' | meh-dee-NAW | meh-dee-NA)_

### Definition

from H1777; properly, a judgeship, i.e., jurisdiction; by implication, a district (as ruled by a judge); generally, a region; (× every) province; n-f

- province, district
  - district
  - province
