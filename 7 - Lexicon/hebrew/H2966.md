# H2966 טְרֵפָה / טרפה

## ṭᵉrêphâh

_(ter-ay-faw' | teh-ray-PAW | teh-ray-FA)_

### Definition

feminine (collectively) of H2964; prey, i.e., flocks devoured by animals; ravin, (that which was) torn (of beasts, in pieces); n-f

- that which is torn, animal torn (by beasts)
