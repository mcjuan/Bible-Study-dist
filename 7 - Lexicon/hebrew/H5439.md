# H5439 סָבִיב / סביב

## çâbîyb

_(saw-beeb' | saw-BEEB | sa-VEEV)_

### Definition

or (feminine) סְבִיבָה; from H5437; (as noun) a circle, neighbour, or environs; but chiefly (as adverb, with or without preposition) around; (place, round) about, circuit, compass, on every side; adv

- (subst) places round about, circuit, round about
- (adv) in a circuit, a circuit, round about
- (prep) in the circuit, from every side
