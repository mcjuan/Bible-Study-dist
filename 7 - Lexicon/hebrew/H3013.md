# H3013 יָגָה / יגה

## yâgâh

_(yaw-gaw' | yaw-ɡAW | ya-ɡA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to grieve; afflict, cause grief, grieve, sorrowful, vex; v

- to afflict, grieve, suffer, cause grief
  - (Niphal) grieved, grieving (participle)
  - (Piel) to grieve
  - (Hiphil) to cause grief, cause sorrow
