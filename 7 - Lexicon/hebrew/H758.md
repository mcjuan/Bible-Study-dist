# H758 אֲרָם / ארם

## ʼĂrâm

_(arawm' | uh-RAWM | uh-RAHM)_

### Definition

exalted; from the same as H0759; the highland; Aram or Syria, and its inhabitants; also the name of the son of Shem, a grandson of Nahor, and of an Israelite; Aram, Mesopotamia, Syria, Syrians; n-pr-m n-m

- (n pr m) Aram or Syria the nation
- the Syrian or Aramean people
- (n m) fifth son of Shem
- a grandson of Nahor
- a descendant of Asher
