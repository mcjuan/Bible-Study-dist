# H6172 עֶרְוָה / ערוה

## ʻervâh

_(er-vaw' | er-WAW | er-VA)_

### Definition

from H6168; nudity, literally (especially the pudenda) or figuratively (disgrace, blemish); nakedness, shame, unclean(-ness); n-f

- nakedness, nudity, shame, pudenda
  - pudenda (implying shameful exposure)
  - nakedness of a thing, indecency, improper behavior
  - exposed, undefended (figuratively)
