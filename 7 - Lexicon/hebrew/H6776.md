# H6776 צֶמֶד / צמד

## tsemed

_(tseh'-med | tseh-MED | tseh-MED)_

### Definition

H6775; a yoke or team (i.e., pair); hence, an acre (i.e., day's task for a yoke of cattle to plough); acre, couple, × together, two (donkeys), yoke (of oxen); n-m

- couple, pair, team, yoke
  - couple, pair, span (usually of animals)
  - acre
    - the amount of land a span of oxen could plow in one day
