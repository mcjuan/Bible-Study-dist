# H7503 רָפָה / רפה

## râphâh

_(raw-faw' | raw-PAW | ra-FA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to slacken (in many applications, literal or figurative); abate, cease, consume, draw (toward evening), fail, (be) faint, be (wax) feeble, forsake, idle, leave, let alone (go, down), (be) slack, stay, be still, be slothful, (be) weak(-en); v

- to sink, relax, sink down, let drop, be disheartened
  - (Qal)
    - to sink down
    - to sink, drop
    - to sink, relax, abate
    - to relax, withdraw
  - (Niphal) idle (participle)
  - (Piel) to let drop
  - (Hiphil)
    - to let drop, abandon, relax, refrain, forsake
    - to let go
    - to refrain, let alone
    - to be quiet
  - (Hithpael) to show oneself slack
