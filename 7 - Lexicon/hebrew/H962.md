# H962 בָּזַז / בזז

## bâzaz

_(baw-zaz' | baw-DZAHDZ | ba-ZAHZ)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to plunder; catch, gather, (take) for a prey, rob(-ber), spoil, take (away, spoil), × utterly; v

- to spoil, plunder, prey upon, seize
  - (Qal) to spoil, plunder, despoil
  - (Niphal) to be spoiled, plundered
  - (Pual) to be taken as spoil
