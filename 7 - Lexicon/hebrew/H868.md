# H868 אֶתְנַן / אתנן

## ʼethnan

_(eth-nan' | et-NAHN | et-NAHN)_

### Definition

the same as H0866; a gift (as the price of harlotry or idolatry); hire, reward; n-m

- hire of prostitute, price
  - hire (of harlot)
  - of idolatrous Israel, Jerusalem, Tyre (figuratively)
