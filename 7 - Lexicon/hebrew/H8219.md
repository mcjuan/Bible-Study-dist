# H8219 שְׁפֵלָה / שפלה

## shᵉphêlâh

_(shef-ay-law' | sheh-pay-LAW | sheh-fay-LA)_

### Definition

from H8213; Lowland, i.e., (with the article) the maritime slope of Palestine; low country, (low) plain, vale(-ley); n-f

- lowland, valley
  - lowland
    - strip west of Judean mountains (technical term)
      - the Shephelah
    - strip near coast north of Carmel
