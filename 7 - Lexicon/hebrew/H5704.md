# H5704 עַד / עד

## ʻad

_(ad | ad | ad)_

### Definition

properly, the same as H5703 (used as a preposition, adverb or conjunction; especially with a preposition); as far (or long, or much) as, whether of space (even unto) or time (during, while, until) or degree (equally with); against, and, as, at, before, by (that), even (to), for(-asmuch as), (hither-)to, + how long, into, as long (much) as, (so) that, till, toward, until, when, while, (+ as) yet; prep

- (prep) as far as, even to, until, up to, while, as far as
  - of space
    - as far as, up to, even to
  - in combination
    - from...as far as, both...and (with 'min'—from)
  - of time
    - even to, until, unto, till, during, end
  - of degree
    - even to, to the degree of, even like
- (conj) until, while, to the point that, so that even
