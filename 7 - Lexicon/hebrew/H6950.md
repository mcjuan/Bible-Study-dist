# H6950 קָהַל / קהל

## qâhal

_('kaw-hal' | kaw-HAHL | ka-HAHL)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to convoke; assemble (selves) (together), gather (selves) (together); v

- to assemble, gather
  - (Niphal) to assemble
    - for religious reasons
    - for political reasons
  - (Hiphil) to summon an assembly
    - for war, judgment
    - for religious purposes
