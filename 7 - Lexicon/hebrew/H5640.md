# H5640 סָתַם / סתם

## çâtham

_(saw-tham' | saw-TAHM | sa-TAHM)_

### Definition

or שָׂתַם; (Numbers 24:15), a primitive root; to stop up; by implication, to repair; figuratively, to keep secret; closed up, hidden, secret, shut out (up), stop; v

- to stop up, shut up, keep close
  - (Qal)
    - to stop up
    - to shut up, keep close
    - secret (participle)
  - (Niphal) to be stopped up
  - (Piel) to stop up
