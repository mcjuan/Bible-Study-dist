# H5997 עָמִית / עמית

## ʻâmîyth

_(aw-meeth' | aw-MEET | ah-MEET)_

### Definition

from a primitive root meaning to associate; companionship; hence (concretely) a comrade or kindred man; another, fellow, neighbour; n-m

- relation, neighbour, associate, fellow
