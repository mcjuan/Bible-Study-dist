# H805 אֲשׁוּרִי / אשורי

## ʼĂshûwrîy

_(ash-oo-ree' | uh-shoo-REE | uh-shoo-REE)_

### Definition

guided, blessed, steps; or אַשּׁוּרִי; from a patrial word of the same form as H0804; an Ashurite (collectively) or inhabitant of Ashur, a district in Palestine; Asshurim, Ashurites; a n-pr-m

- a tribe inhabiting the plain of Esdraelon
- (n pr m) a tribe descended from Abraham and Keturah
