# H6006 עָמַס / עמס

## ʻâmaç

_(aw-mas' | aw-MAHS | ah-MAHS)_

### Definition

or עָמַשׂ; a primitive root; to load, i.e., impose a burden (or figuratively, infliction); be borne, (heavy) burden (self), lade, load, put; v

- to load, carry, carry a load
  - (Qal)
    - to load
    - to carry a load
  - (Hiphil) to lay a load on
