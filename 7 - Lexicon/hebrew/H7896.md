# H7896 שִׁית / שית

## shîyth

_(sheeth | sheet | sheet)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to place (in a very wide application); apply, appoint, array, bring, consider, lay (up), let alone, × look, make, mark, put (on), + regard, set, shew, be stayed, × take; v

- to put, set
  - (Qal)
    - to put, lay (hand upon)
    - to set, station, appoint, fix, set mind to
    - to constitute, make (one something), make like, perform
    - to take one's stand
    - to lay waste
  - (Hophal) to be imposed, be set upon
