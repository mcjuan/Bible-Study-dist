# H6466 פָּעַל / פעל

## pâʻal

_(paw-al' | paw-AL | pa-AL)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to do or make (systematically and habitually), especially to practise; commit, (evil-)do(-er), make(-r), ordain, work(-er); v

- to do, make
  - (Qal)
    - to do
    - to make
