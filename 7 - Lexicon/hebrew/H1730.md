# H1730 דּוֹד / דוד

## dôwd

_(dode | dode | dode)_

### Definition

or (shortened) דֹּד; from an unused root meaning properly, to boil, i.e; (figuratively) to love; by implication, a love-token, lover, friend; specifically an uncle; (well-)beloved, father's brother, love, uncle; n-m

- beloved, love, uncle
  - loved one, beloved
  - uncle
  - love (pl. abstract)
