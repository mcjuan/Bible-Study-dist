# H8486 תֵּימָן / תימן

## têymân

_(tay-mawn' | tay-MAWN | tay-MAHN)_

### Definition

or תֵּמָן; denominative from H3225; the south (as being on the right hand of a person facing the east); south (side, -ward, wind); n-f

- south, southward, whatever is on the right (so the southern quarter), south wind
  - south (of territory)
  - southern quarter (of the sky)
  - toward the south, southward (of direction)
  - south wind
