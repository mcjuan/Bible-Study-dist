# H1265 בְּרוֹשׁ / ברוש

## bᵉrôwsh

_(ber-osh' | beh-ROHSH | beh-ROHSH)_

### Definition

of uncertain derivation; a cypress (?) tree; hence, a lance or a musical instrument (as made of that wood); fir (tree); n-m

- cypress, fir, juniper, pine
  - a noble tree (lit.)
  - of stateliness (figuratively)
  - material for temple
