# H5582 סָעַד / סעד

## çâʻad

_(saw-ad' | saw-AD | sa-AD)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to support (mostly figurative); comfort, establish, hold up, refresh self, strengthen, be upholden; v

- to support, sustain, stay, establish, strengthen, comfort
  - (Qal) to support, sustain
    - to sustain, stay (of heart)
    - to support, uphold (of throne)
