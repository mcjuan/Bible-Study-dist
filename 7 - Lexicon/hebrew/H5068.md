# H5068 נָדַב / נדב

## nâdab

_(naw-dab' | naw-DAHB | na-DAHV)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to impel; hence, to volunteer (as a soldier), to present spontaneously; offer freely, be (give, make, offer self) willing(-ly); v

- to incite, impel, make willing
  - (Qal) to incite, impel
  - (Hithpael)
    - to volunteer
    - to offer free-will offerings
