# H6872 צְרוֹר / צרור

## tsᵉrôwr

_(tser-ore' | tseh-RORE | tseh-RORE)_

### Definition

or (shorter) צְרֹר; from H6887; a parcel (as packed up); also a kernel or particle (as if a package); bag, × bendeth, bundle, least grain, small stone; n-m

- bundle, parcel, pouch, bag (as packed)
- pebble
