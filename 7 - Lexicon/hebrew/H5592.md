# H5592 סַף / סף

## çaph

_(saf | sahp | sahf)_

### Definition

from H5605, in its original sense of containing; a vestibule (as a limit); also a dish (for holding blood or wine); bason, bowl, cup, door (post), gate, post, threshold; n-m

- a spreading out, basin, goblet, bowl
  - basin, goblet
- threshold, sill
  - threshold, sill, door-keeper
