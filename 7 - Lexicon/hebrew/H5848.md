# H5848 עָטַף / עטף

## ʻâṭaph

_(aw-taf' | aw-TAHP | ah-TAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to shroud, i.e., clothe (whether transitive or reflexive); hence (from the idea of darkness) to languish; cover (over), fail, faint, feebler, hide self, be overwhelmed, swoon; v

- to turn aside
  - (Qal) to turn aside, turn (in order to cover)
- to envelop oneself
  - (Qal) to envelop oneself, cover
- to be feeble, be faint, grow weak
  - (Qal) to be feeble, be faint
  - (Niphal) to faint
  - (Hiphil) to show feebleness
  - (Hithpael) to faint, faint away
