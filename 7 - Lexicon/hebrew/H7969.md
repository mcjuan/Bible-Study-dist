# H7969 שָׁלוֹשׁ / שלוש

## shâlôwsh

_(shaw-loshe' | shaw-LOHSH | sha-LOHSH)_

### Definition

or שָׁלֹשׁ; masculine שְׁלוֹשָׁה; or שְׁלֹשָׁה; a primitive number; three; occasionally (ordinal) third, or (multiple) thrice; + fork, + often(-times), third, thir(-teen, -teenth), three, + thrice; n

- three, triad
  - 3, 300, third

Compare H7991.
