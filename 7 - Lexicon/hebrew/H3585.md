# H3585 כַּחַשׁ / כחש

## kachash

_(kakh'-ash | ka-HAHSH | ka-HAHSH)_

### Definition

from H3584; literally a failure of flesh, i.e., emaciation; figuratively, hypocrisy; leanness, lies, lying; n-m

- lying, deception
- leanness, failure
