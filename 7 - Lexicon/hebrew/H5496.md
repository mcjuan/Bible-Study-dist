# H5496 סוּת / סות

## çûwth

_(sooth | soot | soot)_

### Definition

perhaps denominative from H7898; properly, to prick, i.e., (figuratively) stimulate; by implication, to seduce; entice, move, persuade, provoke, remove, set on, stir up, take away; v

- to incite, allure, instigate, entice
  - (Hiphil)
    - to incite (to a request)
    - to allure, lure
    - to instigate (bad sense)
