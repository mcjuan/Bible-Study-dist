# H7980 שָׁלַט / שלט

## shâlaṭ

_(shaw-lat' | shaw-LAHT | sha-LAHT)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to dominate, i.e., govern; by implication, to permit; (bear, have) rule, have dominion, give (have) power; v

- to domineer, exercise power over, dominate, have mastery, be master, lord it over
  - (Qal) to domineer, lord it over, become master
  - (Hiphil)
    - to give power of
    - to get mastery of
