# H2397 חָח / חח

## châch

_(khawkh | haw | hahk)_

### Definition

once (Ezekiel 29:4) חָחִי; from the same as H2336; a ring for the nose (or lips); bracelet, chain, hook; n-m

- hook, ring, fetter, brooch
  - hook, ring (in nose of captive)
  - hook, ring (as jewellery)
    - nose ring
    - bracelet
