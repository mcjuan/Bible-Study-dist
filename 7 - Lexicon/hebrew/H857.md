# H857 אָתָה / אתה

## ʼâthâh

_(aw-thaw' | aw-TAW | ah-TA)_

### Definition

or אָתָא; a primitive root (collateral to H0225 contraction); to arrive; (things to) (be-)come (upon), bring; v

- to come, arrive
  - (Qal) to come (of men, time, beasts, calamity)
  - (Hiphil) to bring
