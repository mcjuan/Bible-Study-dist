# H8159 שָׁעָה / שעה

## shâʻâh

_(shaw-aw' | shaw-AW | sha-AH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to gaze at or about (properly, for help); by implication, to inspect, consider, compassionate, be nonplussed (as looking around in amazement) or bewildered; depart, be dim, be dismayed, look (away), regard, have respect, spare, turn; v

- to look at or to, regard, gaze at or about
  - (Qal) to gaze at, regard, behold, look about
  - (Hiphil) to look away, cause gaze to turn away
  - (Hithpael) to look in dismay, gaze about (in anxiety)
