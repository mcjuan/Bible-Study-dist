# H7298 רַהַט / רהט

## rahaṭ

_(rah'-hat | ra-HAHT | ra-HAHT)_

### Definition

from an unused root apparently meaning to hollow out; a channel or watering-box; by resemblance a ringlet of hair (as forming parallel lines); gallery, gutter, trough; n-m

- trough, hollow
- lock of hair
  - meaning dubious
