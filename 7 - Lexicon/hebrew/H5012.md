# H5012 נָבָא / נבא

## nâbâʼ

_(naw-baw' | naw-BAW | na-VA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to prophesy, i.e., speak (or sing) by inspiration (in prediction or simple discourse); prophesy(-ing), make self a prophet; v

- to prophesy
  - (Niphal)
    - to prophesy
      - under influence of divine spirit
      - of false prophets
  - (Hithpael)
    - to prophesy
      - under influence of divine spirit
      - of false prophets
