# H4268 מַחֲסֶה / מחסה

## machăçeh

_(makh-as-eh' | ma-huh-SEH | ma-huh-SEH)_

### Definition

or מַחְסֶה; from H2620; a shelter (literally or figuratively); hope, (place of) refuge, shelter, trust; n-m

- refuge, shelter
  - from rain or storm, from danger
  - of falsehood
