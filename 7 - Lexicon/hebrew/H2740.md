# H2740 חָרוֹן / חרון

## chârôwn

_(khaw-rone' | haw-RONE | ha-RONE)_

### Definition

or (shortened) חָרֹן; from H2734; a burning of anger; sore displeasure, fierce(-ness), fury, (fierce) wrath(-ful); n-m

- anger, heat, burning (of anger)
  - always used of God's anger
