# H662 אָפַק / אפק

## ʼâphaq

_(aw-fak' | aw-PAHK | ah-FAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to contain, i.e., (reflexive) abstain; force (oneself), restrain; v

- to hold, be strong, restrain, hold back
  - (Hithpael)
    - to restrain oneself, refrain
    - to force, compel oneself
