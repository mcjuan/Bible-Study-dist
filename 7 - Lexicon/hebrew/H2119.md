# H2119 זָחַל / זחל

## zâchal

_(zaw-khal' | dzaw-HAHL | za-HAHL)_

### Definition

a primitive root; by implication, to fear; be afraid, serpent, worm; v

- to shrink back, crawl away
  - (Qal) crawling, creeping (participle) (metonymy)
- to fear, be afraid
