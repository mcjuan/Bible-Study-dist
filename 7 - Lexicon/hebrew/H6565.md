# H6565 פָּרַר / פרר

## pârar

_(paw-rar' | paw-RAHR | pa-RAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to break up (usually figuratively), i.e., to violate, frustrate; × any ways, break (asunder), cast off, cause to cease, × clean, defeat, disannul, disappoint, dissolve, divide, make of none effect, fail, frustrate, bring (come) to nought, × utterly, make void; v

- to break, frustrate
  - (Hiphil)
    - to break, violate
    - to frustrate, make ineffectual
  - (Hophal)
    - to be frustrated
    - to be broken
    - to break
  - (Pilpel) to break to bits, shatter
- to split, divide
  - (Qal) to split, crack through
  - (Poel) to break apart
  - (Hithpoel) to be split, be cracked through
