# H7410 רָם / רם

## Râm

_(rawm | rawm | rahm)_

### Definition

high, exalted; active participle of H7311; high; Ram, the name of an Arabian and of an Israelite; Ram; n-pr-m

- a Judaite, son of Hezron, father of Amminadab, and ancestor of David
- a Judaite, son of Jerahmeel
- a kindred of Elihu, the friend of Job
