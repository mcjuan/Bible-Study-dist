# H7194 קָשַׁר / קשר

## qâshar

_(kaw-shar' | kaw-SHAHR | ka-SHAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to tie, physically (gird, confine, compact) or mentally (in love, league); bind (up), (make a) conspire(-acy, -ator), join together, knit, stronger, work (treason); v

- to bind, tie, bind together, league together, conspire
  - (Qal)
    - to bind, confine
    - to league together, conspire
  - (Niphal) to be bound, be bound up
  - (Piel)
    - to bind on
    - to bind fast
    - to bind, tie
    - to bind to oneself
  - (Pual) robust, vigorous (participle)
  - (Hithpael) to conspire
