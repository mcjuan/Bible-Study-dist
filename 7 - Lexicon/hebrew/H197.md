# H197 אוּלָם / אולם

## ʼûwlâm

_(oo-lawm' | oo-LAWM | oo-LAHM)_

### Definition

or (shortened), אֻלָם ; from H0481 (in the sense of tying); a vestibule (as bound to the building); porch; n-m

- porch
  - in Solomon's temple
  - in Solomon's palace
  - in temple of Ezekiel's vision
