# H8150 שָׁנַן / שנן

## shânan

_(shaw-nan' | shaw-NAHN | sha-NAHN)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to point (transitive or intransitive); intensively, to pierce; figuratively, to inculcate; prick, sharp(-en), teach diligently, whet; v

- to sharpen, whet
  - (Qal) to whet, sharpen
  - (Piel) to sharpen, teach (incisively)
  - (Hithpolel) to be pierced
