# H5287 נָעַר / נער

## nâʻar

_(naw-ar' | naw-AR | na-AR)_

### Definition

a primitive root (probably identical with H5286, through the idea of the rustling of mane, which usually accompanies the lion's roar); to tumble about; shake (off, out, self), overthrow, toss up and down; v

- to shake, shake out or off
  - (Qal) to shake out, show emptiness
  - (Niphal)
    - to be shaken
    - to shake oneself
  - (Piel) to shake off or out
  - (Hithpael) to shake oneself
