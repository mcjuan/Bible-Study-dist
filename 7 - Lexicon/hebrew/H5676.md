# H5676 עֵבֶר / עבר

## ʻêber

_(ay'-ber | ay-BER | ay-VER)_

### Definition

from H5674; properly, a region across; but used only adverbially (with or without a preposition) on the opposite side (especially of the Jordan; ususally meaning the east); × against, beyond, by, × from, over, passage, quarter, (other, this) side, straight; n-m

- region beyond or across, side
  - region across or beyond
  - side, opposite side
