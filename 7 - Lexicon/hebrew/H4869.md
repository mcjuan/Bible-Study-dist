# H4869 מִשְׂגָּב / משגב

## misgâb

_(mis-gawb' | mis-ɡAWB | mees-ɡAHV)_

### Definition

height; from H7682; Misgab, a place in Moab; defence, high fort (tower), refuge; n-m

- (n m) high place, refuge, secure height, retreat
  - stronghold
  - refuge (of God)
- (n pr loc) a place in Moab
