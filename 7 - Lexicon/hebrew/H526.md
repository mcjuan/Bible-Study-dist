# H526 אָמוֹן / אמון

## ʼÂmôwn

_(aw-mone' | aw-MONE | ah-MONE)_

### Definition

skilled workman, master workman; the same as H0525; Amon, the name of three Israelites; Amon; n-pr-m

- a king of Judah, son of Manasseh
- a governor of Samaria
- a descendant of a servant of Solomon
