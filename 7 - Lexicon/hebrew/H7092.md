# H7092 קָפַץ / קפץ

## qâphats

_(kaw-fats' | kaw-PAHTS | ka-FAHTS)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to draw together, i.e., close; by implication, to leap (by contracting the limbs); specifically, to die (from gathering up the feet); shut (up), skip, stop, take out of the way; v

- to draw together, close, shut, shut up, stop up
  - (Qal) to shut
  - (Niphal)
    - to draw oneself together (of death)
    - to be shut up to death
  - (Piel) springing, skipping (participle)
