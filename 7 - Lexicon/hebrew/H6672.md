# H6672 צֹהַר / צהר

## tsôhar

_(tso'-har | tsoh-HAHR | tsoh-HAHR)_

### Definition

from H6671; a light (i.e., window); dual double light, i.e., noon; midday, noon(-day, -tide), window; n-m

- (n m) noon, midday
  - noon (as specific time of day)
  - noon (in simile as bright of happiness, blessing)
- (n f) roof
  - meaning dubious
