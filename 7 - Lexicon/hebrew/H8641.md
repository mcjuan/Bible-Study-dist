# H8641 תְּרוּמָה / תרומה

## tᵉrûwmâh

_(ter-oo-maw' | teh-roo-MAW | teh-roo-MA)_

### Definition

or תְּרֻמָה; (Deuteronomy 12:11), from H7311; a present (as offered up), especially in sacrifice or as tribute; gift, heave offering (shoulder), oblation, offered(-ing); n-f

- contribution, offering
  - a heave offering
  - any offering
  - an offering to God
  - an offering (of grain, money, etc)
  - contribution
