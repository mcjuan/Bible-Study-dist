# H6116 עֲצָרָה / עצרה

## ʻătsârâh

_(ats-aw-raw' | uh-tsaw-RAW | uh-tsa-RA)_

### Definition

or עֲצֶרֶת; from H6113; an assembly, especially on a festival or holiday; (solemn) assembly (meeting); n-f

- assembly, solemn assembly
  - assembly (sacred or festive meeting)
  - assemblage, company, group
