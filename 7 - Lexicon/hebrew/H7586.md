# H7586 שָׁאוּל / שאול

## Shâʼûwl

_(shaw-ool' | shaw-OOL | sha-OOL)_

### Definition

desired; passive participle of H7592; asked; Shaul, the name of an Edomite and two Israelites; Saul, Shaul; n-pr-m

- a Benjamite, son of Kish, and the 1st king of Israel
- an early king of Edom and a successor of Samlah
- a son of Simeon
- a Levite, son of Uzziah
