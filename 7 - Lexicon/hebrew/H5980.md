# H5980 עֻמָּה / עמה

## ʻummâh

_(oom-maw' | oom-MAW | oo-MA)_

### Definition

from H6004; conjunction, i.e., society; mostly adverb or preposition (with prepositional prefix), near, beside, along with; (over) against, at, beside, hard by, in points; n-f

- juxtaposition
  - used only as a prep
    - close by, side by side with, alongside of, parallel with
    - agreeing with, corresponding to, exactly as, close beside
    - correspondingly to
