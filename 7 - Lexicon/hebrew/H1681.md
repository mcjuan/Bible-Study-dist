# H1681 דִּבָּה / דבה

## dibbâh

_(dib-baw' | dib-BAW | dee-BA)_

### Definition

from H1680 (in the sense of furtive motion); slander; defaming, evil report, infamy, slander; n-f

- whispering, defamation, evil report
  - whispering
  - defamation, defaming
  - evil report, unfavorable saying
