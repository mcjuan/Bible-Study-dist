# H5291 נַעֲרָה / נערה

## naʻărâh

_(nah-ar-aw' | na-uh-RAW | na-uh-RA)_

### Definition

feminine of H5288; a girl (from infancy to adolescence); damsel, maid(-en), young (woman); n-f

- girl, damsel, female servant
  - girl, damsel, little girl
    - of young woman, marriageable young woman, concubine, prostitute
  - maid, female attendant, female servant
