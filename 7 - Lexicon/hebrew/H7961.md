# H7961 שָׁלֵו / שלו

## shâlêv

_(shaw-lave' | shaw-LAY | sha-LAVE)_

### Definition

or שָׁלֵיו; feminine שְׁלֵוָה; from H7951; tranquil; (in a bad sense) careless; abstractly, security; (being) at ease, peaceable, (in) prosper(-ity), quiet(-ness), wealthy; a

- quiet, at ease, prosperous
  - at ease (of persons)
  - quiet (of land)
  - quiet, ease
  - ease (subst)
