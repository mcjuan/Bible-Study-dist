# H8251 שִׁקּוּץ / שקוץ

## shiqqûwts

_(shik-koots' | shik-KOOTS | shee-KOOTS)_

### Definition

or שִׁקֻּץ; from H8262; disgusting, i.e., filthy; especially idolatrous or (concretely) an idol; abominable filth (idol, -ation), detestable (thing); n-m

- detestable thing or idol, abominable thing, abomination, idol, detested thing
