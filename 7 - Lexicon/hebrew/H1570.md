# H1570 גָּלַשׁ / גלש

## gâlash

_(gaw-lash' | ɡaw-LAHSH | ɡa-LAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; probably to caper (as a goat); appear; v

- to sit, sit up, (possibly also) recline
  - (Qal)
    - of flocks of goats
    - of a woman's hair (simile)
