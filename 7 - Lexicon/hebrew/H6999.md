# H6999 קָטַר / קטר

## qâṭar

_(kaw-tar' | kaw-TAHR | ka-TAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of fumigation in a close place and perhaps thus driving out the occupants); to smoke, i.e., turn into fragrance by fire (especially as an act of worship); burn (incense, sacrifice) (upon), (altar for) incense, kindle, offer (incense, a sacrifice); v

- (v) to sacrifice, burn incense, burn sacrifices, make sacrifices smoke
  - (Piel)
    - to make sacrifices smoke
    - to sacrifice
  - (Pual) to smoke a sacrifice
  - (Hiphil)
    - to make sacrifices smoke
    - to cause incense to smoke, offer incense
    - to make smoke upon
  - (Hophal) to be made to smoke
- (n m) incense
- (n f) incense-altar
