# H6643 צְבִי / צבי

## tsᵉbîy

_(tseb-ee' | tseh-BEE | tseh-VEE)_

### Definition

from H6638 in the sense of prominence; a gazelle (as beautiful); beautiful(-ty), glorious(-ry), goodly, pleasant, roe(-buck); n-m

- beauty, glory, honor
  - beauty, decoration
  - honor
- roebuck, gazelle
  - perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning unknown
