# H4732 מִקְלוֹת / מקלות

## Miqlôwth

_(mik-lohth' | mik-LOTE | meek-LOTE)_

### Definition

staves; (or perhaps mik-kel-ohth') plural of (feminine) H4731; rods; Mikloth, a place in the Desert; Mikloth; n-pr-m

- one of the sons of Jehiel, the father or prince of Gibeon by his wife Maachah; a Benjamite
- leader of the 2nd division of David's army
