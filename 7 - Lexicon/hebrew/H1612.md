# H1612 גֶּפֶן / גפן

## gephen

_(gheh'-fen | ɡeh-PEN | ɡeh-FEN)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to bend; a vine (as twining), especially the grape; vine, tree; n-m

- vine, vine tree
  - of Israel (figuratively)
  - of stars fading at Jehovah's judgment (metaphorically)
  - of prosperity
