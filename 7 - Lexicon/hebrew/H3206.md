# H3206 יֶלֶד / ילד

## yeled

_(yeh'-led | yeh-LED | yeh-LED)_

### Definition

from H3205; something born, i.e., a lad or offspring; boy, child, fruit, son, young man (one); n-m

- child, son, boy, offspring, youth
  - child, son, boy
  - child, children
  - descendants
  - youth
  - apostate Israelites (figuratively)
