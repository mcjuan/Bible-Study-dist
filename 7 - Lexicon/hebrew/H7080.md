# H7080 קָסַם / קסם

## qâçam

_(kaw-sam' | kaw-SAHM | ka-SAHM)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to distribute, i.e., determine by lot or magical scroll; by implication, to divine; divine(-r, -ation), prudent, soothsayer, use (divination); v

- (Qal) to practice divination, divine
  - of diviners of the nations, Balaam
  - of false prophets of Israel
  - prohibited
