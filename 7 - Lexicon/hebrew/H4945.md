# H4945 מַשְׁקֶה / משקה

## mashqeh

_(mash-keh' | mahsh-KEH | mahsh-KEH)_

### Definition

from H8248; properly, causing to drink, i.e., a butler; by implication (intransitively), drink (itself); figuratively, a well-watered region; butler(-ship), cupbearer, drink(-ing), fat pasture, watered; n-m

- irrigation, drink
  - irrigation
  - drink, drinking vessels
  - butlership (office of butler)
- butler, cup-bearer
