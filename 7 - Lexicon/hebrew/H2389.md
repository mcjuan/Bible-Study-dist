# H2389 חָזָק / חזק

## châzâq

_(khaw-zawk' | haw-DZAWK | ha-ZAHK)_

### Definition

from H2388; strong (usu. in a bad sense, hard, bold, violent); harder, hottest, impudent, loud, mighty, sore, stiff(-hearted), strong(-er); a

- strong, stout, mighty
  - strong
    - severe, sharp, hot
    - firm, hard
  - a strong one (subst)
