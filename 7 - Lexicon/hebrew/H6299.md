# H6299 פָּדָה / פדה

## pâdâh

_(paw-daw' | paw-DAW | pa-DA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to sever, i.e., ransom; generally to release, preserve; × at all, deliver, × by any means, ransom, (that are to be, let be) redeem(-ed), rescue, × surely; v

- to ransom, redeem, rescue, deliver
  - (Qal) to ransom
  - (Niphal) to be ransomed
  - (Hiphil) to allow one to be ransomed
  - (Hophal) redeemed
