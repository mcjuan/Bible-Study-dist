# H48 אֲבִירָם / אבירם

## ʼĂbîyrâm

_(ab-ee-rawm' | uh-bee-RAWM | uh-vee-RAHM)_

### Definition

my father is exalted, [the] Exalted One is [my] father; from H0001 and H7311; father of height (i.e., lofty); Abiram, the name of two Israelites; Abiram; n-pr-m

- a Reubenite, son of Eliab in exodus
- son of Hiel the Bethelite who worked to rebuild Jericho
