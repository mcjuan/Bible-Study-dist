# H2247 חָבָה / חבה

## châbâh

_(khaw-bah' | haw-BAW | ha-VA)_

### Definition

a primitive root (compare H2245); to secrete; hide (self); v

- to withdraw, hide, hide oneself
  - (Qal) to withdraw
  - (Niphal) to hide oneself, remain hidden, withdraw
