# H8055 שָׂמַח / שמח

## sâmach

_(saw-makh' | saw-MA | sa-MAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root; probably to brighten up, i.e., (figuratively) be (causatively, make) blithe or gleesome; cheer up, be (make) glad, (have, make) joy(-ful), be (make) merry, (cause to, make to) rejoice, × very; v

- to rejoice, be glad
  - (Qal)
    - to rejoice
    - to rejoice (arrogantly), exult (at)
    - to rejoice (religiously)
  - (Piel) to cause to rejoice, gladden, make glad
  - (Hiphil) to cause to rejoice, gladden, make glad
