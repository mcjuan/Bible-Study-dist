# H14 אָבָה / אבה

## ʼâbâh

_(aw-baw' | aw-BAW | ah-VA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to breathe after, i.e., (figuratively) to be acquiescent; consent, rest content will, be willing; v

- to be willing, consent
  - (Qal)
    - to be willing
    - to consent, yield to, accept
    - to desire
