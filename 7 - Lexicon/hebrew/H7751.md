# H7751 שׁוּט / שוט

## shûwṭ

_(shoot | shoot | shoot)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to push forth; (but used only figuratively) to lash, i.e., (the sea with oars) to row; by implication, to travel; go (about, through, to and fro), mariner, rower, run to and fro; v

- to go, go or rove about, go to and fro
  - (Qal) to go or rove about
  - (Polel) to go to and fro, go eagerly or quickly to and fro
  - (Hithpolel) to run to and fro
- to row
