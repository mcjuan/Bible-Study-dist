# H481 אָלַם / אלם

## ʼâlam

_(aw-lam' | aw-LAHM | ah-LAHM)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to tie fast; hence (of the mouth) to be tongue-tied; bind, be dumb, put to silence; v

- to bind
  - (Niphal)
    - to be dumb
    - to be bound
  - (Piel) binding (part.)
