# H4970 מָתַי / מתי

## mâthay

_(maw-thah'ee | maw-TAI | ma-TAI)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to extend; properly, extent (of time); but used only adverbially (especially with other particle prefixes), when (either relative or interrogative); long, when; adv

- when?
  - with prep
    - against when?, until when?, how long?, after how long?
