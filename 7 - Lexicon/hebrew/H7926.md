_(shek-em' | sheh-KEM | sheh-HEM)_

### Definition

from H7925; the neck (between the shoulders) as the place of burdens; figuratively, the spur of a hill; back, × consent, portion, shoulder; n-m

- shoulder, back
  - shoulder, shoulder-blade
