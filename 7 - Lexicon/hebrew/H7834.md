# H7834 שַׁחַק / שחק

## shachaq

_(shakh'-ak | sha-HAHK | sha-HAHK)_

### Definition

from H7833; a powder (as beaten small); by analogy, a thin vapor; by extension, the firmament; cloud, small dust, heaven, sky; n-m

- dust, cloud
  - fine dust
  - (thin) cloud
