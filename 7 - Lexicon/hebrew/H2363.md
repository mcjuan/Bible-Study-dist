# H2363 חוּשׁ / חוש

## chûwsh

_(koosh | hoosh | hoosh)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to hurry; figuratively, to be eager with excitement or enjoyment; (make) haste(-n), ready; v

- to haste, make haste, hurry
  - (Qal) to make haste
  - (Hiphil)
    - to show haste, act quickly, hasten, come quickly
    - to enjoy, be excited
