# H681 אֵצֶל / אצל

## ʼêtsel

_(ay'-tsel | ay-TSEL | ay-TSEL)_

### Definition

from H0680 (in the sense of joining); a side; (as a preposition) near; at, (hard) by, (from) (beside), near (unto), toward, with; n-m

- beside, by, near, nearness, joining, proximity
- (subst)
  - conjunction, proximity
  - beside, in proximity, contiguous to, from beside
