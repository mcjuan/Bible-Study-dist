# H724 אֲרוּכָה / ארוכה

## ʼărûwkâh

_(ar-oo-kaw' | uh-roo-KAW | uh-roo-HA)_

### Definition

or אֲרֻכָה; feminine passive participle of H0748 (in the sense of restoring to soundness); wholeness (literally or figuratively); health, made up, perfected; n-f

- healing, restoration
