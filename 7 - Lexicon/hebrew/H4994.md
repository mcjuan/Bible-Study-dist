# H4994 נָא / נא

## nâʼ

_(naw | naw | na)_

### Definition

a primitive particle of incitement and entreaty, which may usually be rendered; 'I pray', 'now', or 'then'; added mostly to verbs (in the Imperative or Future), or to interjections, occasionally to an adverb or conjunction; I beseech (pray) thee (you), go to, now, oh; inj

- I (we) pray, now, please
  - used in entreaty or exhortation
