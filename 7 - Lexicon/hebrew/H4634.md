## maʻărâkâh

_(mah-ar-aw-kaw' | ma-uh-raw-KAW | ma-uh-ra-HA)_

### Definition

feminine of H4633; an arrangement; concretely, a pile; specifically a military array; army, fight, be set in order, ordered place, rank, row; n-f

- row, rank, battle-line
  - battle-line
  - rank
  - row
