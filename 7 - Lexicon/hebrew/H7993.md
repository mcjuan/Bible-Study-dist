# H7993 שָׁלַךְ / שלך

## shâlak

_(shaw-lak | shaw-LAHK | sha-LAHK)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to throw out, down or away (literally or figuratively); adventure, cast (away, down, forth, off, out), hurl, pluck, throw; v

- to throw, cast, hurl, fling
  - (Hiphil)
    - to throw, cast, throw away, cast off, shed, cast down
    - to cast (lots) (figuratively)
  - (Hophal)
    - to be thrown, be cast
    - to be cast forth or out
    - to be cast down
    - to be cast (metaphorically)
