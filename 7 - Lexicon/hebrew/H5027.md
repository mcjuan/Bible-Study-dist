# H5027 נָבַט / נבט

## nâbaṭ

_(naw-bat' | naw-BAHT | na-VAHT)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to scan, i.e., look intently at; by implication, to regard with pleasure, favor or care; (cause to) behold, consider, look (down), regard, have respect, see; v

- to look, regard
  - (Piel) to look
  - (Hiphil)
    - to look
    - to regard, show regard to, pay attention to, consider
    - to look upon, regard, show regard to
