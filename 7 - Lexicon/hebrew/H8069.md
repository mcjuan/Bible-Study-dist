# H8069 שָׁמִיר / שמיר

## Shâmîyr

_(shaw-meer' | shaw-MEER | sha-MEER)_

### Definition

a point, thorn; the same as H8068; Shamir, the name of two places in Palestine; Shamir; n-pr

- (n pr loc) a town in the mountain district of Judah
- a town in Ephraim where the judge Tola lived and was buried

## Note

Compare H8053.
