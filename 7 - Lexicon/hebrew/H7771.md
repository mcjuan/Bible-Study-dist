# H7771 שׁוֹעַ / שוע

## shôwaʻ

_(sho'-ah | SHOH-ah | SHOH-ah)_

### Definition

from H7768 in the original sense of freedom; a noble, i.e., liberal, opulent; also (as noun in the derived sense) a halloo; bountiful, crying, rich; n-m

- (adj) independent, noble, free, rich, generous
- (n m) cry
  - perhaps war-cry, cry for help
