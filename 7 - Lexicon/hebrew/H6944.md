# H6944 קֹדֶשׁ / קדש

## qôdesh

_(ko'-desh | koh-DESH | koh-DESH)_

### Definition

from H6942; a sacred place or thing; rarely abstract, sanctity; consecrated (thing), dedicated (thing), hallowed (thing), holiness, (× most) holy (× day, portion, thing), saint, sanctuary; n-m

- apartness, holiness, sacredness, separateness
  - apartness, sacredness, holiness
    - of God
    - of places
    - of things
  - set-apartness, separateness
