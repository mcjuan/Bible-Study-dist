# H64 אָבֵל כְּרָמִים / אבל כרמים

## ʼÂbêl Kᵉrâmîym

_(aw-bale' ker-aw-meem' | aw-BALE keh-raw-MEEM | ah-VALE keh-ra-MEEM)_

### Definition

meadow of the vineyards; from H0058 and the plural of H3754; meadow of vineyards; Abel-Keramim, a place in Palestine; plain of the vineyards; n-pr-loc

- a place in Ammon
