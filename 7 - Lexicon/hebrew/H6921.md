# H6921 קָדִים / קדים

## qâdîym

_(kaw-deem' | kaw-DEEM | ka-DEEM)_

### Definition

or קָדִם; from H6923; the fore or front part; hence (by orientation) the East (often adverbially, eastward, for brevity the east wind); east(-ward, wind); n-m

- east, east wind
  - east (of direction)
  - east wind
