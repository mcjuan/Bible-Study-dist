# H1731 דּוּד / דוד

## dûwd

_(dood | dood | dood)_

### Definition

from the same as H1730; a pot (for boiling); also (by resemblance of shape) a basket; basket, caldron, kettle, (seething) pot; n-m

- pot, jar, basket, kettle
  - pot, kettle
  - basket, jar
