# H974 בָּחַן / בחן

## bâchan

_(baw-khan' | baw-HAHN | ba-HAHN)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to test (especially metals); generally and figuratively, to investigate; examine, prove, tempt, try (trial); v

- to examine, try, prove
  - (Qal)
    - to examine, scrutinise
    - to test, prove, try (of gold, persons, the heart, man of God)
  - (Niphal) to be tried, proved
  - (Pual) to make a trial
