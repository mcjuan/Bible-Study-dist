# H5172 נָחַשׁ / נחש

## nâchash

_(naw-khash' | naw-HAHSH | na-HAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to hiss, i.e., whisper a (magic) spell; generally, to prognosticate; × certainly, divine, enchanter, (use) × enchantment, learn by experience, × indeed, diligently observe; v

- to practice divination, divine, observe signs, learn by experience, diligently observe, practice fortunetelling, take as an omen
  - (Piel)
    - to practice divination
    - to observe the signs or omens
