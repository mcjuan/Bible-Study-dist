# H60 אֵבֶל / אבל

## ʼêbel

_(ay'-bel | ay-BEL | ay-VEL)_

### Definition

from H0056; lamentation; mourning; n-m

- mourning
  - for the dead
  - for rites of mourning (metaphorically)
  - mourning garb
  - period of mourning
