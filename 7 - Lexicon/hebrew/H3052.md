# H3052 יְהַב / יהב

## yᵉhab

_(yeh-hab' | yeh-HAHB | yeh-HAHV)_

### Definition

corresponding to H3051; to give (whether literal or figurative); generally, to put; imperatively (reflexive) come; deliver, give, lay, + prolong, pay, yield; v

- to give, provide
  - (P'al)
    - to give
    - to place, lay (foundations)
  - (Hithp'al)
    - to be given
    - to be paid
