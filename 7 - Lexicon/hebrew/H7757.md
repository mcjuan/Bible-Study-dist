# H7757 שׁוּל / שול

## shûwl

_(shool | shool | shool)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to hang down; a skirt; by implication, a bottom edge; hem, skirt, train; n-m

- skirt (of robe)
  - of high priest's robe
  - of God's train, city as woman, ignominy, defilement (figuratively)
