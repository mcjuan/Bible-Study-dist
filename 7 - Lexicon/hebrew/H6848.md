# H6848 צֶפַע / צפע

## tsephaʻ

_(tseh'-fah or tsiphoniy tsif-o-nee' | tseh-PA | tseh-FA)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to extrude; a viper (as thrusting out the tongue, i.e., hissing); adder, cockatrice; n-m

- poisonous serpent
  - a viper snake or adder
