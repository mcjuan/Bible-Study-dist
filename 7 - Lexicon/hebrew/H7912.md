## shᵉkach

_(shek-akh' | sheh-KA | sheh-HAHK)_

### Definition

corresponding to H7911 through the idea of disclosure of a covered or forgotten thing; to discover (literally or figuratively); find; v

- to find
  - (Aphel) to find
  - (Ithp'al) to be found
