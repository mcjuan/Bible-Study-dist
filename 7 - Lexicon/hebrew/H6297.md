# H6297 פֶּגֶר / פגר

## peger

_(peh'gher | peh-ɡER | peh-ɡER)_

### Definition

from H6296; a carcase (as limp), whether of man or beast; figuratively, an idolatrous image; carcase, corpse, dead body; n-m

- corpse, carcass, monument, stela
  - corpse (of man)
  - carcass (of animals)
