# H6252 עַשְׁתָּרוֹת / עשתרות

## ʻAshtârôwth

_(ash-taw-roth' | ash-taw-ROTE | ash-ta-ROTE)_

### Definition

star; or עַשְׁתָּרֹת; plural of H6251; Ashtaroth, the name of a Sidonian deity, and of a place East of the Jordan; Asharoth, Astaroth; n-pr-f

- (n pr f deity) false goddesses in the Canaanite religion, usually related to fertility cult
- (n pr loc) a city in Bashan east of the Jordan given to Manasseh
  - same as H6255
