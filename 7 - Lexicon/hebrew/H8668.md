# H8668 תְּשׁוּעָה / תשועה

## tᵉshûwʻâh

_(tesh-oo-aw' | teh-shoo-AW | teh-shoo-AH)_

### Definition

or תְּשֻׁעָה; from H7768 in the sense of H3467; rescue (literal or figurative, persons, national or spiritual); deliverance, help, safety, salvation, victory; n-f

- salvation, deliverance
  - deliverance (usually by God through human agency)
  - salvation (spiritual in sense)
