# H5350 נִקֻּד / נקד

## niqqud

_(nik-kood' | nik-KOOD | nee-KOOD)_

### Definition

from the same as H5348; a crumb (as broken to spots); also a biscuit (as pricked); cracknel, mouldy; n-m

- crumbled thing, thing easily crumbled, crumbs
  - crumbs
  - apparently a kind of hard biscuit or cake
