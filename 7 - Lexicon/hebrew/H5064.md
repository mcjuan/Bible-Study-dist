# H5064 נָגַר / נגר

## nâgar

_(naw-gar' | naw-ɡAHR | na-ɡAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to flow; figuratively, to stretch out; causatively, to pour out or down; figuratively, to deliver over; fall, flow away, pour down (out), run, shed, spilt, trickle down; v

- to pour, run, flow, pour down
  - (Niphal)
    - to be poured, be spilt
    - to pour oneself, flow, trickle
    - to vanish (figuratively)
    - to be stretched out
  - (Hiphil) to pour down
  - (Hophal) to melt
