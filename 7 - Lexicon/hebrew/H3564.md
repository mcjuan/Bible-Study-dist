# H3564 כּוּר / כור

## kûwr

_(koor | koor | koor)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning properly, to dig through; a pot or furnace (as if excavated); furnace; n-m

- (n m) furnace, forge, smelting furnace or pot
- (v) (Qal) to bore, pierce, dig, hew

## Note

Compare H3600.
