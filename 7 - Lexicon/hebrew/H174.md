# H174 אֲהָלִים / אהלים

## ʼăhâlîym

_(a-haw-leem' | uh-haw-LEEM | uh-ha-LEEM)_

### Definition

or (feminine) אֲהָלוֹת (only used thus in the plural); of foreign origin; aloe wood (i.e., sticks); (tree of lign-)aloes; n-m

- aloes, aloe tree
  - aloe tree
  - aloe (perfume)
