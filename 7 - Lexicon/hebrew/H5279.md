# H5279 נַעֲמָה / נעמה

## Naʻămâh

_(nah-am-aw' | na-uh-MAW | na-uh-MA)_

### Definition

loveliness; feminine of H5277; pleasantness; Naamah, the name of a place in Palestine; Naamah; n-pr

- (n pr f) daughter of Lamech by his wife Zillah and sister of Tubal-cain in the days before the flood
- the Ammonite wife of Solomon and mother of king Rehoboam of Judah
- (n pr f loc) a town in the lowlands of Judah in Philistia
