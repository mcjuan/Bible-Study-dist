# H5712 עֵדָה / עדה

## ʻêdâh

_(ay-daw' | ay-DAW | ay-DA)_

### Definition

feminine of H5707 in the original sense of fixture; a stated assemblage (specifically, a concourse, or generally, a family or crowd); assembly, company, congregation, multitude, people, swarm; n-f

- congregation, gathering

## Note

Compare H5713.
