# H5178 נְחֹשֶׁת / נחשת

## nᵉchôsheth

_(nekh-o'-sheth | neh-hoh-SHET | neh-hoh-SHET)_

### Definition

for H5154; copper, hence, something made of that metal, i.e., coin, a fetter; figuratively, base (as compared with gold or silver); brasen, brass, chain, copper, fetter (of brass), filthiness, steel; n-m

- copper, bronze
  - copper (ore), bronze (as copper alloy)
  - fetters (of copper or bronze)
  - copper (as value)
- lust, harlotry
  - meaning dubious
