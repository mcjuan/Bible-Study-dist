# H6895 קָבַב / קבב

## qâbab

_(kaw-bab' | kaw-BAHB | ka-VAHV)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to scoop out, i.e., (figuratively) to malign or execrate (i.e., stab with words); × at all, curse; v

- to curse, utter a curse against
  - (Qal) to curse
