# H6593 פִּשְׁתֶּה / פשתה

## pishteh

_(pish-teh' | pish-TEH | peesh-TEH)_

### Definition

from the same as H6580 as in the sense of comminuting; linen (i.e., the thread, as carded); flax, linen; n-f

- flax, linen
  - fibre used as material for garments
