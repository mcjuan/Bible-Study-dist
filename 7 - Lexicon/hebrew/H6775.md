# H6775 צָמַד / צמד

## tsâmad

_(tsaw-mad' | tsaw-MAHD | tsa-MAHD)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to link, i.e., gird; figuratively, to serve, (mentally) contrive; fasten, frame, join (self); v

- to bind, join, fasten
  - (Niphal) to join, join or attach oneself to
  - (Pual) to be fastened, be bound
  - (Hiphil) to combine, fit together, hitch up
