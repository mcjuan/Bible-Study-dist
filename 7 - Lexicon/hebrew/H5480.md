# H5480 סוּךְ / סוך

## çûwk

_(sook | sook | sook)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to smear over (with oil), i.e., anoint; anoint (self), × at all; v

- to anoint, pour in anointing
  - (Qal) to anoint
    - to anoint oneself
    - to anoint (another)
    - to be poured
  - (Hiphil) to anoint
