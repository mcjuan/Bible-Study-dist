# H521 אַמָּה / אמה

## ʼammâh

_(am-maw' | am-MAW | ah-MA)_

### Definition

corresponding to H0520; properly, a mother (i.e., unit of measure, or the fore-arm (below the elbow), i.e., a cubit; also a door-base (as a bond of the entrance); cubit; n

- cubit—a measure of distance (the forearm), roughly 18 in (.5 m)
