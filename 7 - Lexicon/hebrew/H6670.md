# H6670 צָהַל / צהל

## tsâhal

_(tsaw-hal' | tsaw-HAHL | tsa-HAHL)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to gleam, i.e., (figuratively) be cheerful; bellow, cry aloud (out), lift up, neigh, rejoice, make to shine, shout; v

- to neigh, cry shrilly
  - (Qal)
    - to neigh (figurative for men as stallions)
    - (Piel) to cry shrilly (in distress)
- (Hiphil) to make shining
