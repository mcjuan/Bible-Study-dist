# H6793 צִנָּה / צנה

## tsinnâh

_(tsin-naw' | tsin-NAW | tsee-NA)_

### Definition

feminine of H6791; a (large) shield (as if guarding by prickliness); buckler, cold, hook, shield, target; n-f

- something piercing, hook, barb
  - meaning dubious
- coolness, cold (of snow)
- shield, large shield, buckler
  - shield
