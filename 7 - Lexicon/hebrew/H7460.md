# H7460 רָעַד / רעד

## râʻad

_(raw-ad' | raw-AD | ra-AD)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to shudder (more or less violently); tremble; v

- to tremble, quake
  - (Qal)
    - to tremble
    - (earth)quake
  - (Hiphil) trembling (participle)
