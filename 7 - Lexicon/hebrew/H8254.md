# H8254 שָׁקַל / שקל

## shâqal

_(shaw-kal' | shaw-KAHL | sha-KAHL)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to suspend or poise (especially in trade); pay, receive(-r), spend, × throughly, weigh; v

- to weigh, weigh out, pay out
  - (Qal)
    - to weigh
    - to weigh out (a price)
    - of grief (figuratively)
  - (Niphal)
    - to be weighed
    - to be weighed out
