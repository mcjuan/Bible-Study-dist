# H5930 עֹלָה / עלה

## ʻôlâh

_(o-law' | oh-LAW | oh-LA)_

### Definition

or עוֹלָה; feminine active participle of H5927; a step or (collectively, stairs, as ascending); usually a holocaust (as going up in smoke); ascent, burnt offering (sacrifice), go up to; n-f

- whole burnt offering
- ascent, stairway, steps
