# H4832 מַרְפֵּא / מרפא

## marpêʼ

_(mar-pay' | mahr-PAY | mahr-PAY)_

### Definition

from H7495; properly, curative, i.e., literally (concretely) a medicine, or (abstractly) a cure; figuratively (concretely) deliverance, or (abstractly) placidity; (in-)cure(-able), healing(-lth), remedy, sound, wholesome, yielding; n-m

- health, healing, cure
  - healing, cure
  - health, profit, sound (of mind)
  - healing
    - incurable (with negative)
