# H5144 נָזַר / נזר

## nâzar

_(naw-zar' | naw-DZAHR | na-ZAHR)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to hold aloof, i.e., (intransitivey) abstain (from food and drink, from impurity, and even from divine worship (i.e., apostatize)); specifically, to set apart (to sacred purposes), i.e., devote; consecrate, separate(-ing) (self); v

- to dedicate, consecrate, separate
  - (Niphal) to dedicate oneself, devote oneself
  - (Hiphil) to keep sacredly separate
- (Hiphil) to be a Nazarite, live as a Nazarite
