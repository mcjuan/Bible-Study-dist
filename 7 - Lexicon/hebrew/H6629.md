# H6629 צֹאן / צאן

## tsôʼn

_(tsone | tsone | tsone)_

### Definition

or צאוֹן; (Psalm 144:13), from an unused root meaning to migrate; a collective name for a flock (of sheep or goats); also figuratively (of men); (small) cattle, flock, + flocks, lamb, + lambs, sheep(-cote, -fold, -shearer, -herds); n-f

- small cattle, sheep, sheep and goats, flock, flocks
  - small cattle (usually of sheep and goats)
  - of multitude (simile)
  - of multitude (metaphor)
