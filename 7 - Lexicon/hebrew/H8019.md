# H8019 שְׁלֹמִית / שלמית

## Shᵉlômîyth

_(shel-o-meeth' | sheh-loh-MEET | sheh-loh-MEET)_

### Definition

peaceful; or שׁלוֹמִית; (Ezra 8:10), from H7965; peaceableness; Shelomith, the name of three Israelitesses; Shelomith; n-pr-m m-pr-f

- (n pr m) a Gershonite Levite, son of Zichri in the time of David
  - also 'Shelomoth'; same as H8013
- son of king Rehoboam of Judah by Maacah the granddaughter of Absalom
- head of a family of exiles who returned with Ezra
- (n pr f) a woman of Dan, daughter of Dibri, and mother of a son who blasphemed God and was stoned to death in the time of Moses
- daughter of Zerubbabel
