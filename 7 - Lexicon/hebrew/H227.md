# H227 אָז / אז

## ʼâz

_(awz | awdz | az)_

### Definition

a demonstrative adverb; at that time or place; also as a conjunction, therefore; beginning, for, from, hitherto, now, of old, once, since, then, at which time, yet; adv

- then, at that time
  - temporal expressions
    - then (past)
    - then, if...then (future)
    - earlier
  - logical expressions
    - in that case
    - that (being so)
