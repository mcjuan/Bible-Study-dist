# H8081 שֶׁמֶן / שמן

## shemen

_(sheh'-men | sheh-MEN | sheh-MEN)_

### Definition

from H8080; grease, especially liquid (as from the olive, often perfumed); figuratively, richness; anointing, × fat (things), × fruitful, oil(-ed), ointment, olive, + pine; n-m

- fat, oil
  - fat, fatness
  - oil, olive oil
    - as staple, medicament or unguent
    - for anointing
  - fat (of fruitful land, valleys) (metaphorically)
