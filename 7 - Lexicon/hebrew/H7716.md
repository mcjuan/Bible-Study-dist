# H7716 שֶׂה / שה

## seh

_(seh | seh | seh)_

### Definition

or שֵׂי; probably from H7582 through the idea of pushing out to graze; a member of a flock, i.e., a sheep or goat; (lesser, small) cattle, ewe, goat, lamb, sheep; n-m

- one of a flock, lamb, sheep, goat, young sheep, young goat
  - sheep, goat
  - flock (collective)

## Note

Compare H2089.
