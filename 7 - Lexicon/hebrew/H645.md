# H645 אֵפוֹ / אפו

## ʼêphôw

_(ay-fo' | ay-POH | ay-FOH)_

### Definition

or אֵפוֹא; from H6311; strictly a demonstrative particle, here; but used of time, now or then; here, now, where?; prt

- then, now, so
  - (who) then, (what) then (with interrog)
  - then (with imperative—i.e., know then)
  - if...then (with adv)
