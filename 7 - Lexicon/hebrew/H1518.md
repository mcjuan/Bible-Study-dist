# H1518 גִּיחַ / גיח

## gîyach

_(ghee'-akh | ɡEE-ah | ɡEE-ak)_

### Definition

or (shortened) גֹּחַ; a primitive root; to gush forth (as water), generally to issue; break forth, labor to bring forth, come forth, draw up, take out; v

- to burst forth
  - (Qal)
    - to burst forth
    - to draw forth
    - to bring forth
  - (Hiphil) to break forth
