# H56 אָבַל / אבל

## ʼâbal

_(aw-bal' | aw-BAHL | ah-VAHL)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to bewail; lament, mourn; v

- to mourn, lament
  - (Qal) to mourn, lament
    - of humans
    - of inanimate objects (figuratively)
      - of gates
      - of land
  - (Hiphil)
    - to mourn, cause to mourn (figuratively)
  - (Hithpael)
    - to mourn
    - play the mourner
