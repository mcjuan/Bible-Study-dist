# H2403 חַטָּאָה / חטאה

## chaṭṭâʼâh

_(khat-taw-aw' | haht-taw-AW | ha-ta-AH)_

### Definition

or חַטָּאת; from H2398; an offence (sometimes habitual sinfulness), and its penalty, occasion, sacrifice, or expiation; also (concretely) an offender; punishment (of sin), purifying(-fication for sin), sin(-ner) (offering); n-f

- sin, sinful
- sin, sin offering
  - sin
  - condition of sin, guilt of sin
  - punishment for sin
  - sin-offering
  - purification from sins of ceremonial uncleanness
