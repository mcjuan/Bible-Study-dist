# H7153 קִרְיַת אַרְבַּע / קרית ארבע

## Qiryath ʼArbaʻ

_(keer-yath' ar-bah' | kir-YAHT ar-BA | keer-YAHT ar-BA)_

### Definition

city of Arba; or (with the article interposed) קִרְיַת הָאַרְבַּע; (Nehemiah 11:25), from H7151 and H0704 or H0702; city of Arba, or city of the four (giants); Kirjath-Arba or Kirjath-ha-Arba, a place in Palestine; Kirjath-arba; n-pr-loc

- early name of the city which after the conquest was called 'Hebron'
