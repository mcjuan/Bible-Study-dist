# H4046 מַגֵּפָה / מגפה

## maggêphâh

_(mag-gay-faw' | mahg-gay-PAW | ma-ɡay-FA)_

### Definition

from H5062; a pestilence; by analogy, defeat; (× be) plague(-d), slaughter, stroke; n-f

- blow, slaughter, plague, pestilence, strike, smite
  - blow (fatal stroke)
  - slaughter (of battle)
  - plague, pestilence (divine judgment)
