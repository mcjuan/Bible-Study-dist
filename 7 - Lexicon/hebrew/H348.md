# H348 אִיזֶבֶל / איזבל

## ʼÎyzebel

_(ee-zeh'-bel | ee-dzeh-BEL | ee-zeh-VEL)_

### Definition

Baal exalts, Baal is husband to, unchaste; from H0336 and H2083; Izebel, the wife of king Ahab; Jezebel; n-pr-f

- queen of Israel, wife of Ahab, daughter of Ethbaal
