# H4069 מַדּוּעַ / מדוע

## maddûwaʻ

_(mad-doo'-ah | mahd-DOO-ah | ma-DOO-ah)_

### Definition

or מַדֻּעַ; from H4100 and the passive participle of H3045; what (is) known?; i.e., (by implication) (adverbially) why?; how, wherefore, why; adv

- why?, on what account?, wherefore?
