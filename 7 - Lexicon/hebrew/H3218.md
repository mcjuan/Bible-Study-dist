# H3218 יֶלֶק / ילק

## yeleq

_(yeh'-lek | yeh-LEK | yeh-LEK)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to lick up; a devourer; specifically, the young locust; cankerworm, caterpillar; n-m

- young locust (early stage of development)
  - caterpillar (as devouring)
