# H6470 פָּעַם / פעם

## pâʻam

_(paw-am' | paw-AM | pa-AM)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to tap, i.e., beat regularly; hence (generally) to impel or agitate; move, trouble; v

- to thrust, impel, push, beat persistently
  - (Qal) to impel
  - (Niphal) to be beaten, be disturbed
  - (Hithpael) to be disturbed
