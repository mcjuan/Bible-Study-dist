# H4751 מַר / מר

## mar

_(mar | mahr | mahr)_

### Definition

or (feminine) מָרָה; from H4843; bitter (literally or figuratively); also (as noun) bitterness, or (adverbially) bitterly; + angry, bitter(-ly, -ness), chafed, discontented, × great, heavy; a-m

- (adj) bitter, bitterness
  - of water or food
  - of harlot's end, end of wickedness, cry (figuratively)
  - of pain (subst)
- (adv) bitterly
