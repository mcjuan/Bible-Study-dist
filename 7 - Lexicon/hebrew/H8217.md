# H8217 שָׁפָל / שפל

## shâphâl

_(shaw-fawl' | shaw-PAWL | sha-FAHL)_

### Definition

from H8213; depressed, literally or figuratively; base(-st), humble, low(-er, -ly); a

- low, humble
  - low (in height)
  - low (in station), humble (of condition or spirit)
  - humiliated
  - lowly (as subst)
