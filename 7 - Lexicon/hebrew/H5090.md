# H5090 נָהַג / נהג

## nâhag

_(naw-hag' | naw-HAHɡ | na-HAHɡ)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to drive forth (a person, an animal or chariot), also (from the panting induced by effort), to sigh; acquaint, bring (away), carry away, drive (away), lead (away, forth), (be) guide, lead (away, forth); v

- to drive, lead, guide, conduct
  - (Qal)
    - to drive, lead on, drive away, drive off
    - to behave itself (figuratively) (of heart)
  - (Piel)
    - to drive away, lead off
    - to lead on, guide, guide on
    - to cause to drive
- (Piel) to moan, lament
