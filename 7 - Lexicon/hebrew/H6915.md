# H6915 קָדַד / קדד

## qâdad

_(kaw-dad' | kaw-DAHD | ka-DAHD)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to shrivel up, i.e., contract or bend the body (or neck) in deference; bow (down) (the) head, stoop; v

- (Qal) to bow down
