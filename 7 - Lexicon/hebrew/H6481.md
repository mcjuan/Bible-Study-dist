# H6481 פָּצַע / פצע

## pâtsaʻ

_(paw-tsah' | paw-TSA | pa-TSA)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to split, i.e., wound; wound; v

- to bruise, wound, wound by bruising
  - (Qal) to wound by crushing
