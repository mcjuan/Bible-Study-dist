# H5996 עַמִּישַׁדַּי / עמישדי

## ʻAmmîyshadday

_(am-mee-shad-dah'ee | am-mee-shahd-DAI | ah-mee-sha-DAI)_

### Definition

my kinsman is Almighty; from H5971 and H7706; people of (the) Almighty; Ammishaddai, an Israelite; Ammishaddai; n-pr-m

- father of Ahiezer, prince of the tribe of Dan at the time of the exodus
