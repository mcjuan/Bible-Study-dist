# H5146 נֹחַ / נח

## Nôach

_(no'-akh | NOH-ah | NOH-ak)_

### Definition

rest; the same as H5118; rest; Noach, the patriarch of the flood; Noah; n-pr-m

- son of Lamech, father of Shem, Ham, and Japheth; builder of the ark which saved his family from the destruction of the world which God sent on the world by the flood; became the new seminal head of the human race because his family were the only survivors of the flood
