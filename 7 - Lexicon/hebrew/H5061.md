# H5061 נֶגַע / נגע

## negaʻ

_(neh'-gah | neh-ɡA | neh-ɡA)_

### Definition

from H5060; a blow (figuratively, infliction); also (by implication) a spot (concretely, a leprous person or dress); plague, sore, stricken, stripe, stroke, wound; n-m

- stroke, plague, disease, mark, plague spot
  - stroke, wound
  - stroke (metaphorical of disease)
  - mark (of leprosy)
