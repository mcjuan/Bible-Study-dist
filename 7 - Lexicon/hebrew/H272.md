# H272 אֲחֻזָּה / אחזה

## ʼăchuzzâh

_(akh-ooz-zaw' | uh-hoodz-ZAW | uh-hoo-ZA)_

### Definition

feminine passive participle from H0270; something seized, i.e., a possession (especially of land); possession; n-f

- possession, property
  - land
  - possession by inheritance
