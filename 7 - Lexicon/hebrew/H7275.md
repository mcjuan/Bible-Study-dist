# H7275 רָגַם / רגם

## râgam

_(raw-gam' | raw-ɡAHM | ra-ɡAHM)_

### Definition

a primitive root (compare H7263, H7321, H7551); to cast together (stones), i.e., to lapidate; × certainly, stone; v

- to stone, slay or kill by stoning
  - (Qal) to stone
