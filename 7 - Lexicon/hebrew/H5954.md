# H5954 עֲלַל / עלל

## ʻălal

_(al-al' | uh-LAHL | uh-LAHL)_

### Definition

corresponding to H5953 (in the sense of thrusting oneself in); to enter; causatively, to introduce; bring in, come in, go in; v

- to thrust in, go in, come in
  - (P'al) to go in, come in
  - (Aphel) to bring in
  - (Hophal) to be brought in
