# H6298 פָּגַשׁ / פגש

## pâgash

_(paw-gash' | paw-ɡAHSH | pa-ɡAHSH)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to come in contact with, whether by accident or violence; figuratively, to concur; meet (with, together); v

- to meet, join, encounter
  - (Qal) to meet, encounter
  - (Niphal) to meet together, meet each other
  - (Piel) to meet, encounter
