# H1892 הֶבֶל / הבל

## hebel

_(heh'bel | heh-BEL | heh-VEL)_

### Definition

or (rarely in the abs.) הֲבֵל; from H1891; emptiness or vanity; figuratively, something transitory and unsatisfactory; often used as an adverb; × altogether, vain, vanity; n-m

- vapour, breath
  - breath, vapour
  - vanity (figuratively)
- (adv) vainly
