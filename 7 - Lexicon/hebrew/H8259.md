# H8259 שָׁקַף / שקף

## shâqaph

_(shaw-kaf' | shaw-KAHP | sha-KAHF)_

### Definition

a primitive root; properly, to lean out (of a window), i.e., (by implication) peep or gaze (passively, be a spectacle); appear, look (down, forth, out); v

- to overlook, look down or out, overhang, look out and down
  - (Niphal) to lean over (and look), look down
  - (Hiphil) to look down, look down upon
