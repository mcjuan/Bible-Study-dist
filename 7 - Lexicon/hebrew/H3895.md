# H3895 לְחִי / לחי

## lᵉchîy

_(lekh-ee' | leh-HEE | leh-HEE)_

### Definition

from an unused root meaning to be soft; the cheek (from its fleshiness); hence, the jaw-bone; cheek (bone), jaw (bone); n-m

- jaw, cheek
  - jaw, jawbone (of animal)
  - cheek (of man)
