# H1638 גָּרַס / גרס

## gâraç

_(gaw-ras' | ɡaw-RAHS | ɡa-RAHS)_

### Definition

a primitive root; to crush; also (intransitively and figuratively) to dissolve; break; v

- to be crushed, be broken
  - (Qal) to be crushed
  - (Hiphil) to crush, break (the teeth)
