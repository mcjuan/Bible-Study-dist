## cheres

_(kheh'-res | heh-RES | heh-RES)_

### Definition

a collateral form mediating between H2775 and H2791; a piece of pottery; earth(-en), (pot-)sherd, + stone; n-m

- earthenware, clay pottery, shard, potsherd, earthen vessel
